class=\"section_content\" id=\"abstract\">\n        <img style=\"float:right;margin:8px\" src=\"icon.jpg\">\n<p>We are interested in algorithms that can find objects in images and\nvideo.  Such algorithms have practical implications for applications\nsuch as image search and robotic navigation.  Important and open\nissues include representation and learning - what makes a good model\nof an object, and how can one learn such a representation? To help\nanswer such questions, we employ both generative and discriminative\nmachine-learning techniques.</p>\n\n      </div>\n    </div>\n    <div class=\"section\">\n      <div
