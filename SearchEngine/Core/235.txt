Non-majors are allowed accounts\r\n          only if enrolled in a course under the Bren School of ICS (ICS, CS,\r\n          CSE, Statistics, or Informatics course).</p>\r\n        <hr>\r\n        <p>Take your UCI Photo Student ID Card and see the Lab Assistant in the\r\n          ICS computer Lab (CS 364). Ask the Lab Assistant to verify your identity\r\n          by handing them your student ID card so that you can obtain an ICS instructional\r\n          account.</p>\r\n        <blockquote>\r\n          <p> <em>* If you do not have an ID card, you may present other form\r\n            of picture ID if you know your ID number.</em></p>\r\n          <p> <em>** If you are enrolled through Extension please go to CS 346L\r\n            and ask to see the Lab Manager regarding your account. You will need\r\n            to show proof of enrollment, and some form of picture ID (such as\r\n            a driver's license).</em></p>\r\n        </blockquote>\r\n        <p>After your card has been idverified by the lab assistant, you will\r\n          be led to one of the Windows machine used for account activation. The\r\n          lab assistant will login to the machine and start up an session for\r\n          you.</p>\r\n        <ul>\r\n          <li>You will be asked to type in your Student ID number. Type in your\r\n            number without any punctuation characters and press <font face=\"Courier New, Courier, mono\">Enter</font>.</li>\r\n          <li>You will then have to enter your name. Type your name as it appears\r\n            on your ID card, in the form <font face=\"Courier New, Courier, mono\">Lastname,Firstname\r\n            Middlename </font> and press <font face=\"Courier New, Courier, mono\">Enter</font>.</li>\r\n          <li>A summary of your account status will appear.</li>\r\n          <li>A document on the Ethical Use of Computing will be displayed. It\r\n            is important that you actually read the document since it contains\r\n            information that you are required to know. Remember that at all times\r\n            you are responsible and accountable for knowing what constitutes ethical\r\n            behavior when using ICS computer systems.</li>\r\n          <li>You will be asked if you have read and understood the document.\r\n            Press <font face=\"Courier New, Courier, mono\">I agree</font> to continue.\r\n            If you do not agree with the policies or have anyquestions, please\r\n            see the Lab Manager in CS346L.</li>\r\n        </ul>\r\n        <p>Once you finished, you will be asked to supply an initial password\r\n          for your account. Please remember this password. </p>\r\n        <ul>\r\n          <li>Do Not use your student ID number, your name, your birthday, your\r\n            girl/boy-friend's name etc. </li>\r\n          <li>Do not use any part of your name or username (ie. if your name is\r\n            Sam, you cannot have S and A next to each other in the password).</li>\r\n          <li>Do not use * in your password</li>\r\n          <li>Passwords must be at least 6 characters long</li>\r\n          <li>Passwords must contain characters from at least 3 of the following\r\n            4 categories:\r\n            <ul>\r\n              <li>English Upper Case Letters A, B, C, ..., Z</li>\r\n              <li>English Lower Case Letters a, b, c, ..., z</li>\r\n              <li>Westernized Arabic Numerals 0, 1, 2, ..., 9</li>\r\n              <li>Non-alphanumeric characters . , ; : &amp; % ! #</li>\r\n            </ul>\r\n          </li>\r\n        </ul>\r\n        <p>If all goes well, your account will be created within 2 hours. Be sure\r\n          to write down your login name so you don't forget it.</p>\r\n      <blockquote>\r\n        <div><em>* If your username does not match your UCInetID, please let us\r\n
