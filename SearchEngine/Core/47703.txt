Roman\" size=\"3\">Oktoberfest is an environment to be experienced &#8212; this is all about German (or Bavarian) passion for Beer.\u00c2\u00a0 I joined the MM 2007 Organizing team for this famous Munich Oktober fest (which always takes place in September).\u00c2\u00a0 In a tent (in our case of Hofbrau Haus) there are thousands of people drinking beer and eating the food that goes with it.\u00c2\u00a0 The most interesting thing in this fest are the waitresses who can carry 12-16 large\u00c2\u00a0 filled beerjugs at a time.\u00c2\u00a0 Truly interesting site.\u00c2\u00a0 Other environment is same as in many other festivals all across the world &#8212; lots of people enjoying food and drinks in the company of their friends.\u00c2\u00a0 A somewhat different thing about this fest is that young, middle aged and old people are all together.\u00c2\u00a0 And people even bring their kids in strollers.</font></p>\n<p><font face=\"Times New Roman\" size=\"3\">\u00c2\u00a0</font></p>\n<p><font face=\"Times New Roman\" size=\"3\">It was an experience to be at this festival.\u00c2\u00a0<a title=\"myflickr\"
