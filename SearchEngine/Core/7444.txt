Below is a list of student organizations within the Donald Bren School of ICS.&#160; </p>\n<p><a href=\"http://student-council.ics.uci.edu/\" target=\"_blank\" title=\"ICS Student Council\">ICS Student Council</a></p>\n<p style=\"padding-left: 30px;\">The ICS Student Councl is the official student government for the Donald Bren School of\nInformation and Computer Sciences. Their aim is to improve the lives of students\nacademically, professionally and socially.</p>\n<p><a href=\"http://www.math.uci.edu/%7eicamp/\" target=\"_blank\" title=\"iCAMP\">iCAMP</a> </p>\n<p style=\"padding-left: 30px;\">Interdisciplinary Computation and Applied Mathematics Program</p>\n<p style=\"padding-left: 30px;\">iCAMP provides an opportunity for undergrads to learn about a topic in\ncomputational applied mathematics. Opportunities are also available for paid\nsummer research.</p>\n<p><a href=\"http://wics.ics.uci.edu/\" target=\"_blank\" title=\"WiCS\">WICS</a> </p>\n<p style=\"padding-left: 30px;\">Women in Computer Sciences</p>\n<p style=\"padding-left: 30px;\">By joining WICS, female students can surround themselves with their\npeers and positive influences who, not only work with and can assist them with\ntheir classes and curriculum, but can also help them network with other\nsuccessful women, giving them even more opportunities to succeed.</p>\n<p><strong>ACM </strong></p>\n<p style=\"padding-left: 30px;\">Association for Computing Machinery, Student Chapter</p>\n<p style=\"padding-left: 30px;\">The Association for Computing Machinery is the world's largest society\nof information technology students and professionals. The chapter represents\nthe needs of the UCI computing community.</p>\n<p><strong>INSA </strong></p>\n<p style=\"padding-left: 30px;\">Informatics Student Association</p>\n<p style=\"padding-left: 30px;\">INSA works to unite the Informatics community at UCI and to promote the\nInformatics major to students and companies alike. We offer workshops,\ncorporate visits and tours, individual tutoring and mentoring, tournaments and\ncompetitions and networking/recruiting events with technology companies.</p>\n<p><strong>UCF </strong></p>\n<p style=\"padding-left: 30px;\">Undergraduate Computing Federation</p>\n<p style=\"padding-left: 30px;\">Promotes interaction between and provide resources to students with an\ninterest in Unix, Linux, FreeBSD, Network Security, Cryptography, or Ethical\nHacking. Open to all UCI undergraduate or graduate students.</p>\n<p><strong><a href=\"http://www.igda.org/\" target=\"_blank\" title=\"IGDA\">IGDA</a> </strong></p>\n<p style=\"padding-left: 30px;\">International Game Developers Association, OC Chapter</p>\n<p><strong><a href=\"http://www.maissuci.weebly.com\" target=\"_blank\" title=\"MAISS\">MAISS</a></strong><a href=\"http://www.maissuci.weebly.com\" target=\"_blank\" title=\"MAISS\"></a> </p>\n<p style=\"padding-left: 30px;\">Management Information Student Society</p>\n<p style=\"padding-left: 30px;\">MAISS\nis tailored to the interests of BIM majors at UC Irvine as well as those\ninterested in Business &amp; Technology. On such a heavily-populated campus,\nit's often difficult to find peers in the same major who go through the same\njoys and struggles as you do. With Business Information Management (BIM) majors\nfew and far between, the need for a tight-knit community is even greater. At\nMAISS, we bring together BIM majors to share the journey of personal growth and\nprofessional development. You'll be able to connect with other BIM majors and\ncreate lifelong friendships in the process. MAISS recognizes the challenges\nthat BIM students face and strives to expose BIM students to various resources\nthat will equip them for the real world post-graduation.</p>\n\n\n</div>\n</blockquote>\n</div>\n<div id=\"sidebar\"><div id=\"sidebar_title\">More Undergraduate &#187;</div>\n<ul>\n<li><a href=\"../sao/index.php\">Academic advising</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"../courses/index.php\">Academic year plan</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"../degrees/index.php\">ICS majors</a>\n<ul>\n<li><a href=\"../degrees/Double_Majors.php\">Double major</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"../degrees/Second_BA.php\">2nd baccalaureate</a></li>\n</ul>\n</li>\n<li><a href=\"../degrees/Minors.php\">ICS minors</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"../QA_Petitions.php\">Petitions</a></li>\n<li><span style=\"font-family: Verdana,Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif;
