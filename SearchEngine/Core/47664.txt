\t\t\t<p><font face=\"Times New Roman\" size=\"3\">The first day at the conference was very interesting. \u00c2\u00a0The day started with a 30 minute opening session and had two key note talks. \u00c2\u00a0There were lots of papers in all aspects of multimedia ranging from retrieval to coding. \u00c2\u00a0Suddenly there seems to be a surge in interest in multiview coding. \u00c2\u00a0There was a tutorial also on this topic and several papers in this area here.</font></p>\n<p><font face=\"Times New Roman\" size=\"3\">The first keynote was by Ya-Qin Zhang on Mobile computing.\u00c2\u00a0 He is the corporate vice president of Microsoft and is the director of Microsoft\u00e2\u20ac\u2122s R&#038;D in China. \u00c2\u00a0He outlined what are the challenges in Mobile computing and how Microsoft is dealing with it.\u00c2\u00a0 His talk is <a title=\"YaQin Zhang PCM06\" href=\"http://www.pcm2006.org/Keynotes/yqz.htm\" target=\"_blank\">described here.</a></font></p>\n<p><font face=\"Times New Roman\" size=\"3\">I gave the second keynote talk.\u00c2\u00a0 There was some pressure on me. \u00c2\u00a0From the morning introductory session I have been called \u00e2\u20ac\u02dcthe father of multimedia\u00e2\u20ac\u2122 \u00e2\u20ac\u201c I had people using this term for me informally, but never so openly on stage and in introductions. \u00c2\u00a0This does put you under pressure.\u00c2\u00a0 In any case, I decided to champion the notion of event-based modeling and EventWeb in the talk. \u00c2\u00a0The abstract of the <a title=\"RJ PCM06\" href=\"http://www.pcm2006.org/Keynotes/rj.htm\" target=\"_blank\">talk is here</a>.\u00c2\u00a0 And <a title=\"RJ PCM06\" href=\"http://www.ics.uci.edu/~jain/presentations/PCM%202006%20Event-Based%20MM%20061102.ppt\" target=\"_blank\">the presentation is here</a>.</font></p>\n<p><font size=\"3\"></font><font face=\"Times New Roman\">In the evening there was a nice reception with a great Chinese orchestra.\u00c2\u00a0 </font><font size=\"3\"><font face=\"Times New Roman\" size=\"3\" /></font><font face=\"Times New Roman\" size=\"3\"></font><font face=\"Times New Roman\" size=\"3\">I am posting pictures at flickr.\u00c2\u00a0 You can either search with tag PCM06 or go to my stream at <a
