Diego today, it was clear that San Diego was a better team and lost.\u00c2\u00a0 It was also clear that Tom Brady was not in form still he made his team win.\u00c2\u00a0 In part he was playing terrible &#8212; 3 interceptions.\u00c2\u00a0 Well, the important thing is that he was the reason that New England still won.\u00c2\u00a0 How did this happen &#8212; one thing and the most important thing in sports and other important things in life is mental toughness.\u00c2\u00a0 And I could not help but notice <a title=\"Brady\" mental toughness is not letting anything get to you,&#8221; Brady said after the game. &#8220;It&#8217;s just staying focused not matter what&#8217;s swirling around you, just continuing to mentally fight through whatever obstacles there might be, whether that be a certain play or situation or a bunch of things that come up as a football player. We have a bunch of mentally tough guys. Even though it doesn&#8217;t all go well you still have to believe in yourself and have the confidence that you can go out there and play.&#8221;</p></blockquote>\n<p>There is a good lesson for Indian
