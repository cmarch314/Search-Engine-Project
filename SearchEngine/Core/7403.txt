Applications for a second baccalaureate will not be considered until all required coursework is completed.</p>\n<p><strong>For students whose first degree was from another institution other than UC Irvine:</strong></p>\n<p>Applications will be accepted for the Fall quarter only. Applications must be submitted during the filing period in order to be considered for Fall admission. For detailed information on application procedures, please visit&#160;the <a href=\"http://www.admissions.uci.edu\" target=\"_blank\" title=\"UCI Admissions Office\">Office of Admissions and Relations with Schools</a> website.</p>\n<p><strong>For students whose first degree was completed at UC Irvine:</strong></p>\n<p>Applications will be accepted for any quarter. Former UC Irvine students must meet with an academic counselor to discuss admission for the second baccalaureate. Former UCI students will be admitted via the campus' readmission policy. Students must meet with an ICS academic counselor to process the readmission application. All interested individuals must adhere to the <a href=\"http://www.reg.uci.edu/enrollment/readmission.html\" target=\"_blank\" title=\"Registrars Office: Readmission\">readmission deadlines</a> that are set by the Registrar's Office. </p>\n<p>&#160;</p>\n<hr style=\"width: 100%;\" width=\"100%\"/>\n<p>&#160;</p>\n<p><strong><span style=\"color: #6699cc;\"><strong>&#187;</strong></span> ICS Second Baccalaureate Degrees</strong></p>\n<span style=\"text-decoration: underline;\"><strong>\n</strong></span>\n<ul>\n<li><a href=\"Second_BA_CS.php\" target=\"_blank\" title=\"Computer Science 2nd Baccalaureate\">Computer Science</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"Second_BA_INF.php\" target=\"_blank\" title=\"Informatics 2nd Baccalaureate\">Informatics</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"Second_BA_CSE.php\" target=\"_blank\" title=\"CSE 2nd Baccalaureate\">Computer Science and Engineering</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"Second_BA_SE.php\" target=\"_blank\" title=\"Software Engineering 2nd Baccalaureate\">Software Engineering\n</a></li>\n</ul>\n<br/>\n<p><strong><span style=\"color: #6699cc;\"><strong>&#187;</strong></span> Tips for Second Baccalaureate Applicants</strong></p>\n<p>Applications cannot be considered or activated until all required coursework is completed and relevant transcripts are received.</p>\n<p>&#160;</p>\n<p>Required coursework may be completed at UCI or with equivalent courses taken at an accredited institution. Courses taken at a four-year or two-year institution can be examined by UC Irvine faculty through a petition process to determine equivalency. In order to determine equivalency, a course syllabus/outline with a course description, weekly topics, and a textbook (if necessary) must be submitted to syllabi@ics.uci.edu </p>\n<p>Courses may be taken at UCI through ACCESS UCI via <a href=\"http://www.unex.uci.edu\" target=\"_blank\" title=\"University Extension\">University Extension</a>. For more information about ACCESS and the process, contact an ICS academic counselor.</p>\n<p>Courses taken at universities other than local community colleges should be evaluated for equivalency before submitting your application to ensure you have completed all relevant coursework. Articulation agreements between California community colleges and the Bren School of ICS can be found at <a href=\"http://www.assist.org\">www.assist.org</a>.</p>\n<p>Grade point averages as stated in the policy are required. If you complete several math and computer science courses and find you are not earning high enough grades, you may need to reconsider your decision to apply for a second baccalaureate degree in ICS. You may decide not to finish the required course work if you will not be able to get into ICS. The academic counselors in ICS are available to discuss your chances of being admitted to the program and to help you to identify other options.</p>\n\n\n</div>\n</blockquote>\n</div>\n<div id=\"sidebar\"><div id=\"sidebar_title\">More Undergraduate &#187;</div>\n<ul>\n<li><a href=\"../sao/index.php\">Academic advising</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"../courses/index.php\">Academic year plan</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"index.php\">ICS majors</a>\n<ul>\n<li><a href=\"Double_Majors.php\">Double major</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"Second_BA.php\">2nd baccalaureate</a></li>\n</ul>\n</li>\n<li><a href=\"Minors.php\">ICS minors</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"../QA_Petitions.php\">Petitions</a></li>\n<li><span style=\"font-family: Verdana,Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif;
