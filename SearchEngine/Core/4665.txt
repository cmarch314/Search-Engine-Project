<p>Live demo: <a href=\"http://www.ics.uci.edu/~vpalepu/205project\">http://www.ics.uci.edu/~vpalepu/205project</a></p>\n\n<p>The purpose of this interactive narrative-based tutorial on statistical methods is to establish a simplified way for students to learn statistical concepts (specifically Hypothesis testing with t-tests) that are grounded in real-life examples. In addition to a narrative, an interface in presented that allows students to interact with data sets that are accessible on-line.</p>\n\n<p>The goal is to give students interested in doing research a fun way to quickly and clearly understand critical concepts in statistics, needed to continue to experiment with their own data sets (or on-line ones) so they can interpret future data sets they work with in meaningful and statistically sound ways.</p>\n\n<p><strong>IMPORTANT:</strong> This project was done as a part of a class project in collaboration with Sonny Lin and Dahlia Hegab, at UC Irvine, CA.</p>\n\n</div>\n\n<div><i>TL;DR</i> - Interactive, narrative-based, tutorial for students to learn statistical concepts (specifically Hypothesis testing with t-Tests). </div>\n\n<div style=\"margin-bottom: 10px\">\n\t<br>\n\t<b class=\"label\">Tags:</b>\n\t\n\t\t<span class=\"tags\">statistics</span>\n\t\t\n\t\t\t<span> </span>\n\t\t\n\t\n\t\t<span class=\"tags\">Class Project</span>\n\t\t\n\t\t\t<span> </span>\n\t\t\n\t\n\t\t<span class=\"tags\">Quantitative Methods</span>\n\t\t\n\t\t\t<span> </span>\n\t\t\n\t\n\t\t<span class=\"tags\">hypothesis testing</span>\n\t\t\n\t\t\t<span> </span>\n\t\t\n\t\n\t\t<span class=\"tags\">t-Test</span>\n\t\t\n\t\n</div>\n</div>\n\n<div class=\"plain stretch\">\n  <h2>Related Posts</h2>\n  <ul class=\"posts\">\n    \n      <li><span>11 Jan 2015</span> &raquo;
