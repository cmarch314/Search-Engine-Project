In Daylight semantics,\n<code>R</code><i>n</i> means that an atom is in <i>n</i> rings of the chosen SSSR.\nAs the choice of SSSR is non-deterministic, this interpretation can cause an\narbitrary set of atoms to match depending upon input order.
