class on entrepreneurship yesterday, a student asked whether entrepreneurship be taught or is it that entrepreneurs are born.\u00c2\u00a0 This is the same question as can writing be taught, or can singing be taught, or can Tennis be taught.\u00c2\u00a0 This is a big myth.</p>\n<p>In every field there are some natural talents and characteristics\u00c2\u00a0required.\u00c2\u00a0 And these things one inherits.\u00c2\u00a0 But then there are so many routine things that one learns from other people&#8217;s experience.\u00c2\u00a0 Ultimately what is education &#8212; one aspect of education is sharing experiences of people who converted their experiences to systematic knowledge.\u00c2\u00a0 And in entrepreneurship one can learn so many things from others.\u00c2\u00a0 It is clear that by going to school and taking classes a normal person can not become Einstein or Bill Gates, but most normal people can benefit a lot from classes on any topic related to their interests.</p>\n<p>In any case, I enjoyed the question because I hear this &#8216;myth&#8217; in every area &#8212; I have heard can one be taught &#8216;design&#8217; or &#8230; (name your favorite interest).</p>\n
