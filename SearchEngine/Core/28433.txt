\t\t\t<h1 class=\"entry-title\">\r\n    \t\t\t\tHonored, surprised, and then some thoughts    \t\t\t</h1><!-- .entry-title -->\r\n  \t\t\t</header>\r\n\r\n  \t\t\t\r\n  \t\t\t\r\n  \t\t\t<div class=\"entry-content clearfix\">\r\n    \t\t\t<p>A colleague of mine mentioned that I am listed in a <a href=\"http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_computer_scientists\">List of Computer Scientists on Wikipedia</a>.  I visited the list and must say that I am honored and surprised.  Honored is obvious &#8212; and surprised is because I never took a class in computer science so was not formally trained in computer science, but more importantly, because this list (at this stage) is a relatively short list and finding my name in this list is truly surprising.  </p>\n<p>I don&#8217;t know how these things happen &#8212; and did not even know about my name being on the list, in fact did not know that such a list exists.  What is more important is that many systems in our society are going through transformations &#8212; and one can argue about &#8216;validity&#8217; and implications of those but their existence makes you think about the future.  The system in which a few people formed a &#8216;society&#8217; and that society conferred an honor such as a &#8216;Fellow&#8217; (and luckily I have a fair share of those) was a different mechanism than creating purely in a bottom-up manner a list like this one.  Is this going to become as commonly&#8211; or more &#8212;  used method of recognition?  Time will tell.  I feel that the last few year&#8217;s socio-technical changes are definitely transformative.</p>\n<p>In any case, THANKS to those who made my &#8216;ego&#8217; feel good by putting me on this list.  Now I am more motivated to contribute some ideas that will make me convinced me that I belong in a list like this.</p>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t<div class=\"tags\">\r\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\tTagged on: <a href=\"http://ngs.ics.uci.edu/tag/computer-scientists/\" rel=\"tag\">Computer Scientists</a>, <a href=\"http://ngs.ics.uci.edu/tag/wikipedia/\" rel=\"tag\">wikipedia</a>\t\t\t\t\t\t\t</div>\r\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t  \t\t\t</div>\r\n  \t\t\t<div class=\"entry-meta-bar clearfix\">\t        \t\t\t\r\n    \t\t\t<div class=\"entry-meta\">\r\n    \t\t\t\t<span class=\"by-author\">By <a href=\"http://ngs.ics.uci.edu/author/ramesh/\">Ramesh</a></span> |\r\n    \t\t\t\t<span class=\"date\"><a href=\"http://ngs.ics.uci.edu/honored-surprised-and-started-thinking/\" title=\"11:39 am\">June 19, 2011</a></span> |\r\n    \t\t\t\t             \t\t<span class=\"category\"><a href=\"http://ngs.ics.uci.edu/category/general-updates/\" rel=\"category tag\">General Updates</a></span> |\r\n             \t \r\n    \t\t\t\t             \t\t<span class=\"comments\"><a href=\"http://ngs.ics.uci.edu/honored-surprised-and-started-thinking/#comments\" title=\"Comment on Honored, surprised, and then some thoughts\">1 Comment</a></span> |\r\n             \t \t\t          \t\t\t\t\r\n    \t\t\t</div><!-- .entry-meta -->\r\n    \t\t</div>\r\n\r\n  \t\t\t\t\t\t<ul class=\"default-wp-page clearfix\">\r\n\t\t\t\t<li class=\"previous\"><a href=\"http://ngs.ics.uci.edu/transformation-or-froth-visual-communication/\" rel=\"prev\"><span class=\"meta-nav\">&larr;</span> Transformation or Froth: Visual Communication</a></li>\r\n\t\t\t\t<li class=\"next\"><a href=\"http://ngs.ics.uci.edu/events-are-transparent/\" rel=\"next\">Events are &#8216;transparent&#8217;
