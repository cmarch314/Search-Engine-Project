XTRA communicates\nwith\nthe user in a natural language (German), extracts data relevant to the\nexpert\nsystem from his/her natural-language input, answers user queries as to\nterminology,\nand provides user-accommodated natural-language verbalizations of\nresults\nand explanations provided by the expert system. A number of novel\nartificial\nintelligence techniques have been emplozed in the development of the\nsystem,\nincluding the combination of natural-language user input with user\ngestures\non the terminal screen, referent identification with the aid of four\ndifferent\nknowledge sources, simultaneous communication of the access system with\nthe\nuser and the expert system, fusion of two complementary knowledge bases\ninto\na single one, and the design of a natural-language generation component\nwhich\nallows for a controlled interaction between the \"what-to-say\" and the\n\"how-to-say\"\nparts to yield a more natural output. XTRA is being deveoped\nindependenly\nof any specific expert system.
