The forked process is then overlaid with Geomview. The\nfile descriptors <I>stdin</I> and <I>stdout</I> of Geomview are hooked\non the two pipes.\n<P>\n\nAll insert operators construct expressions in <I>gcl</I>, the Geomview\ncommand language, which is a subset of L<SMALL>ISP</SMALL>. These expressions\nare sent to Geomview via the pipe. The extract operators notify <I>interest</I>\nfor a certain kind of events. When such an event happens Geomview\nsends a description of the event in <I>gcl</I> and the extract operator has\nto parse this expression.\n<P>\n\nIn order to implement further insert and extract operators you should\ntake a look at the implementation and at the Geomview manual.\n<P>\n\n<!Next_reference_link_10_15_2!>\n\n    \n<div class=\"NavBottom\">\n<hr><!-- Bottom Navigation --------------------------------------------- -->\n  <strong>Navigation:</strong>\n<!Up_chapter_link_15!>\n  <a href=\"../contents.html\">Table of Contents</A>,\n  <a href=\"../biblio.html\">Bibliography</a>,\n  <a href=\"../manual_index.html\">Index</a>,\n  <a href=\"../title.html\">Title Page</a>\n<!-- End of Bottom Navigation ------------------------------------------ -->\n\n\n<hr><!-- --------------------------------------------------------------- -->\n<div class=\"NavAddress\">\n\nThe <A HREF=http://www.cgal.org>CGAL Project</A> .\n\nTue, December 21, 2004 .\n<hr></div>\n</body>  </html>  \n", "id": 39064.0}
