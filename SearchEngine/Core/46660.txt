my first day after getting back.  And I spend some of it reading, some of it taking care of routine things and much of it on the Web.<br />\nWhat did people do before the Web?  Oh yes, I remember reading books.  We do that even now also &#8212; but we can do that better on the Web.</p>\n<p>It is a different world when you have access to the Web.  It opens up so many interesting &#8216;viewports&#8217; for you &#8212; you can select what you want and what you like.  Too bad that either many people don&#8217;t realize the importance and many others don&#8217;t have access to it.  Just think how it can help people in developing countries.  In fact, though many people don&#8217;t have access to the Web in developing world &#8212; they are the people who really need it.
