\t\t\t<p>Web2.0 has attracted Attention of several people.  Tim O&#8217;Reilly&#8217;s paper on this topic has been the most &#8216;defining document&#8217;, but still there are so many apects to Web2.0 that this is like that famous &#8216;six blind men and the elephant&#8217; situation.  In my class on entrepreneurism, I gave a simple home work to students to describe what is Web2.0 and its implications to an entrepreneur and the society.   When I got their reports, I decided to collect all the &#8216;buzz words&#8217; that I saw there.  Here are those buzz words &#8212; I am sure you know many of them, if not most or all.  Some of these are their interesting impressions or opinions.  No particular order &#8212; I just scribbled them as I was reading their papers.</p>\n<p>Collective intelligence \u00e2\u20ac\u201c wisdom of crowds<br />\nSoftware as service<br />\nData is King<br
