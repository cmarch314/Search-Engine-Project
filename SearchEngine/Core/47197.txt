graduates.</p>\n<blockquote><p>Premier skills-testing agency MeritTrac has just concluded, after testing 790 B-school graduates in six cities on seven key parameters, that 77 per cent of them are \u00e2\u20ac\u0153unemployable\u00e2\u20ac?. Heads of some top B-schools say this finding doesn\u00e2\u20ac\u2122t surprise them.</p></blockquote>\n<p>This is a major challenge for Indian education system.\u00c2\u00a0 We like to build several elite institutions at the cost of the rest.\u00c2\u00a0 It may be better to raise the overall standard rather than building just a few elite institutions.\u00c2\u00a0 Of course if you can do both then
