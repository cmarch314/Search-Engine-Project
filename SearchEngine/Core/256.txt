ICS Labs\r\n          use a pay-per-sheet printing system that is provided by OIT. Each page\r\n          costs 10 cents to print and you must have a valid print card.\r\n        </p>\r\n\r\n        <ul>\r\n          <li><a href=\"http://www.oit.uci.edu/computing/printing.html\">Where to get a print card</a></li>\r\n          <li><a href=\"#print_doc\">How to send and print documents</a></li>\r\n          <li><a href=\"#\">Getting Refunds</a></li>\r\n        </ul>\r\n\r\n        <hr>\r\n        <h5><a name=\"print_doc\"></a>How to send and print documents</h5>\r\n        <p>Windows:</p>\r\n        <ul>\r\n          <li>Click on <em>print</em> from the application.</li>\r\n          <li>Make sure that the printer name is PayPrint CS364.</li>\r\n          <li>Go to the printer and follow the direction on the print station</li>\r\n          <li>On the screen, find your print job and then hit <em>Print</em>.</li>\r\n          <li>You can continue to print other jobs or click <em>Logout</em> when\r\n            you are done. </li>\r\n          <li>The station will display how much value is left on the card.\r\n            <p>PDF Files - if you are having problems printing a PDF file, please\r\n              make sure that the postscript level is set to 1. To change the level,\r\n              do the following:</p>\r\n            <ul>\r\n              <li>Click on <em>print --&gt; Properties... --&gt; Advanced...</em></li>\r\n              <li>Under <em>Document Options</em>, find <em>PostScript Options</em></li>\r\n              <li>Change <em>PostScript Language Level</em> to 1</li>\r\n            </ul>\r\n            <p>If you find the print job is coming out slowly, try printing a\r\n              couple of pages at a time. Some PDF files are quite large and the\r\n              printer cannot process it all at once.</p>\r\n          </li>\r\n        </ul>\r\n        <p>UNIX:</p>\r\n        <ul>\r\n          <li>ASCII (text) files: <font face=\"Courier New, Courier, mono\">mp</font>\r\n            <em>asciifile.tx</em>t <font face=\"Courier New, Courier, mono\">| lpr\r\n            -Pppcs364</font></li>\r\n          <li>PostScript files: <font face=\"Courier New, Courier, mono\">lpr -Ppcs364</font>\r\n            <em>postscript.ps</em></li>\r\n          <li>TEX DVI files: <font face=\"Courier New, Courier, mono\">dvisp -Pppcs364</font>\r\n            <em>file.dvi</em></li>\r\n        </ul>\r\n        <blockquote>\r\n          <p>For more information, please refer to the <a href=\"http://www.ics.uci.edu/computing/unix/printing.php\">ICS\r\n            Support UNIX Printing Guide</a>.</p>\r\n        </blockquote>\r\n        <h5><a name=\"refund\"></a>Getting Refunds</h5>\r\n        <p>If your print job does not print out properly (ie. streaks, paper jam)\r\n          you can get a refund by contacting the Lab Manager in CS346L. If the\r\n          Lab Manager is not available, please contact <a\r\nhref=\"http://www.oit.uci.edu/org/directions.html\">OIT</a>.\r\n        </p>\r\n        <p>If the print card dispenser malfunctioned and you did not get a card\r\n          or it did not add value to your card, please contact <a\r\nhref=\"http://www.oit.uci.edu/org/directions.html\">OIT</a>.\r\n        </p>\r\n\r\n        <p>&nbsp;
