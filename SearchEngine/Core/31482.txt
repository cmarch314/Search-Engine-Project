Abstract \n<!----------------------------------------------------------------------->\n<H2>Abstract</H2>\n<p>\nThis data consists of 640 black and white face images of people taken with varying pose (straight, left, right, up), expression (neutral, happy, sad, angry), eyes (wearing sunglasses or not), and size.\n</p>\n\n<!------------------------------------------------------------------------\n<!  Sources\n<!----------------------------------------------------------------------->\n<H2> Sources</H2>\n<H4> Original Owner and Donor</H4>\n<PRE>\n<A HREF=\"http://www.cs.cmu.edu/~tom/\">Tom Mitchell</A>\nSchool of Computer Science \nCarnegie Mellon University\n<A HREF=\"mailto:tom.mitchell@cmu.edu\">tom.mitchell@cmu.edu</A>\n</PRE>\n<B>Date Donated: </B> June 24, 1999\n\n<!------------------------------------------------------------------------\n<!  Data Characteristics\n<!----------------------------------------------------------------------->\n<H2> Data Characteristics</H2>\n<P>\nEach image can be characterized by the pose, expression, eyes, and size. There are 32 images for each person capturing every combination of features.\n</P>\n<P>\nShown here are four representative images.\n</P>\n<img src=\"an2i_straight_neutral_open.jpg\" align=\"center\"/>\n<img src=\"boland_right_sad_open.jpg\" align=\"center\"/>\n<img src=\"at33_left_happy_sunglasses.jpg\" align=\"center\"/>\n<img src=\"ch4f_up_angry_sunglasses.jpg\" align=\"center\"/>\n<BR d=\"\"/>\n\n<!------------------------------------------------------------------------\n<!  Other Relevant Information \n<!----------------------------------------------------------------------->\n<H2> Other Relevant Information</H2>\n\n<H4> Viewing the face images</H4>\n\nTo view the images, you can use the program <tt>xv</tt>.  \n\n<!------------------------------------------------------------------------\n<!  Data Format \n<!----------------------------------------------------------------------->\n<H2>Data Format</H2>\n\n<P>The image data can be found in /faces. This directory contains 20 subdirectories, one for each person, named by userid.  Each of these directories contains several different face images of the same person.\n</P>\n<P>\nYou will be interested in the images with the following naming convention:\n</P>\n\n<blockquote>\n  <tt> &lt;userid&gt; &lt;pose&gt; &lt;expression&gt; &lt;eyes&gt; &lt;scale&gt;.pgm </tt>\n</blockquote>\n\n<UL>\n  <li> <tt>&lt;userid&gt;</tt> is the user id of the person in the image, and this field has 20 values: an2i, at33, boland, bpm, ch4f, cheyer, choon, danieln, glickman, karyadi, kawamura, kk49, megak, mitchell, night, phoebe, saavik, steffi, sz24, and tammo. </li>\n  <li> <tt>&lt;pose&gt;</tt> is the head position of the person, and this field has 4 values: straight, left, right, up. </li>\n  <li> <tt>&lt;expression&gt;</tt> is the facial expression of the person, and this field has 4 values: neutral, happy, sad, angry. </li>\n  <li> <tt>&lt;eyes&gt;</tt> is the eye state of the person, and this field has\n2 values: open, sunglasses. </li>\n  <li> <tt>&lt;scale&gt;</tt> is the scale of the image, and this field has 3 values: 1, 2, and 4.  1 indicates a full-resolution image (128 columns by 120 rows); 2 indicates a half-resolution image (64 by 60); 4 indicates a quarter-resolution image (32 by 30).</li>\n</UL>\n\n<P>If you've been looking closely in the image directories, you may notice\nthat some images have a <tt>.bad</tt> suffix rather than the <tt>.pgm</tt>\nsuffix.  As it turns out, 16 of the 640 images taken have glitches due to\nproblems with the camera setup; these are the <tt>.bad</tt> images.\nSome people had more glitches than others, but everyone who got ``faced''\nshould have at least 28 good face images (out of the 32 variations\npossible, discounting scale).\n</P>\n\n\n<!------------------------------------------------------------------------\n<!
