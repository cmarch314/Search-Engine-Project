Students should consider this major if you:\n</p>\n<ul>\n<li>Want to be a productive member of software engineering teams in a variety of application domains including, but not restricted to, Web and mobile applications\n   </li>\n<li>Wish to acquire technical knowledge and have an appreciation for life-long learning\n   </li>\n<li>Are excited by the ability to place software in the social context in which is it developed\n   </li>\n<li>Wish to create novel applications that have the potential to bring social change\n</li>\n</ul>\n<h4>What do I take? </h4>\n<p>\nSee the <a href=\"http://catalogue.uci.edu/donaldbrenschoolofinformationandcomputersciences/#softwareengr\">General Catalogue</a> for a full listing of the degree requirements.
