The organization\nbeneath is a halfedge data structure, which restricts the class of\nrepresentable surfaces to orientable 2-manifolds - with and without\nboundary. If the surface is closed we call it a <I>polyhedron</I>.\n<P>\n\nThe polyhedral surface is realized as a container class managing\nvertices, halfedges, facets with their incidences, and maintaining the\ncombinatorial integrity of them. Its local types for the vertices,\nhalfedges and facets are documented separately.  A default traits\nclass, a default items class and an incremental builder conclude the\nreferences. The polyhedral surface is based on the highly flexible\ndesign of the halfedge data structure, see the reference for\n<I><A
