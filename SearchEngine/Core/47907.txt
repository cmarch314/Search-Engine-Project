Roman\" size=\"3\">I am on a short trip to Switzerland.\u00c2\u00a0 I am visiting IDIAP in Martigny to review a NCCR research project in Interactive Multimodal Information Management.\u00c2\u00a0 The site visit is on Nov. 16 and 17<sup>th</sup> and I will be back on 18<sup>th</sup>.\u00c2\u00a0 I will arrive in Martigny on 15<sup>th</sup> around 7 PM.\u00c2\u00a0 SO there is really absolutely no time to enjoy Switzerland.\u00c2\u00a0 When many of my friends say to me that I get to go to nice places, I want to tell them how \u00e2\u20ac\u02dcinteresting\u00e2\u20ac\u2122 these trips are.\u00c2\u00a0 I will travel about one day to get to the meeting place and after the meeting I will return back.\u00c2\u00a0 These trips are definitely a strong burden on your body and are no fun.\u00c2\u00a0 Of course, you do it for professional reasons.</font></p>\n<p><font face=\"Times New Roman\" size=\"3\">\u00c2\u00a0</font></p>\n<p><font face=\"Times New Roman\" size=\"3\">I am eager to find out the research progress in multimodal aspects.\u00c2\u00a0 It is common that people talk about multimodal but end up doing mostly mono-modal work.\u00c2\u00a0 Reductionist approach to science results in researchers becoming expert in a specific area.\u00c2\u00a0 It is common to find researchers focus on audio or visual information, but not utilizing both.\u00c2\u00a0 Lately there is a push on researchers to really look at multimodal information.\u00c2\u00a0 It is clear that multimodal information simplifies problem solving.\u00c2\u00a0 This will, however, require a slightly different perspective in research.\u00c2\u00a0 So I am eager to see what this leading research group in Switzerland doing to adopt multimodal approaches.</font></p>\n<p><font face=\"Times New Roman\" size=\"3\">\u00c2\u00a0</font></p>\n<p><font size=\"3\"></font><font face=\"Times New Roman\">Switzerland is known for its cleanliness and efficiency.\u00c2\u00a0 Though I have been here numerous times, I am still amazed at how they do it.\u00c2\u00a0 Here the population is not much and they don\u00e2\u20ac\u2122t have the luxury of throwing people to solve problems.\u00c2\u00a0 That only means that people are much disciplined and this must come from early childhood.\u00c2\u00a0 On my way, at LAX I saw part of Federer \u00e2\u20ac\u201c Roddick match in Masters tournament in Shanghai.\u00c2\u00a0 Roger Federer has dominated Tennis for the last few years with his precise perfection of the game.\u00c2\u00a0 The beauty is how easy he makes it look.\u00c2\u00a0 He definitely brings true Swiss spirit with him in his game.</font></p>\n<p><font face=\"Times New Roman\" size=\"3\">\u00c2\u00a0</font></p>\n<p><font face=\"Times New Roman\" size=\"3\">I was expecting it to be colder than it is. \u00c2\u00a0When I landed, Zurich was nice 12 degrees C.\u00c2\u00a0 Not bad for this time of the year.\u00c2\u00a0 Now I am in a train going to Martigny.\u00c2\u00a0 There is a very convenient train directly from the airport to Martigny with a change in Laussanne.\u00c2\u00a0 Total train ride is 3:24 and it will definitely take exactly that much time.\u00c2\u00a0 Everything
