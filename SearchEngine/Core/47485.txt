flat world offers some exciting opportunities to developing countries.\u00c2\u00a0 I was invited to talk in Indian-American Society of San Diego on this topic.\u00c2\u00a0 I have been thinking about this topic so I was only happy to share my ideas and views with people there.\u00c2\u00a0 <a title=\"sandiego talk\" about one hour.\u00c2\u00a0 It was interesting to see perspectives of people &#8212; most people believe that there is a great opportunity, but there are serious challenges also.\u00c2\u00a0 On the other hand, some people appeared to be defensive and did not want to think about the challenges.</p>\n<p>I think that this is the best time India has seen since becoming independent.\u00c2\u00a0 It
