\t\t\t<div class=\"entry-content clearfix\">\r\n    \t\t\t<p><font face=\"Times New Roman\" size=\"3\">Can the concept of wikis can be easily extended to other media \u00e2\u20ac\u201c photos, video, audio, and any other medium?\u00c2\u00a0 Suppose I post a photo.\u00c2\u00a0 By providing suitable editing tools, it may be possible for people to edit this photo to enhance some characteristics of a photo.\u00c2\u00a0 Now this does not sound like a good example of wiki.\u00c2\u00a0 But what about \u00e2\u20ac\u201c I post a raw video collection from an event and give people freedom to edit it so they can create many versions of this video by editing it, maybe by adding background audio or any such thing.\u00c2\u00a0 This could result in a form of video wiki.\u00c2\u00a0 There is no reason that one could create a multimedia wiki like this in which one could combine different media sources to create a message using appropriate media.\u00c2\u00a0 That could be very interesting in many applications.\u00c2\u00a0 But that is a slightly different topic than what I want to talk about today.</font></p>\n<p><font face=\"Times New Roman\" size=\"3\" /></p>\n<p><font size=\"3\" /><font face=\"Times New Roman\">It may be possible to combine the concepts of blog and wiki to develop, what one may call a bliki.\u00c2\u00a0 A bliki will take the most important feature of blog, namely the ability to post what you think is important, and the most important feature of a wiki, namely the consensus authorship or production.\u00c2\u00a0 Like a blog, there could be other features like comments, and other infrastructure that goes with modern blogs.\u00c2\u00a0 Like a wiki, there may be appropriate mechanisms to control the authoring environment to help create a meaningful document or product.\u00c2\u00a0 </font></p>\n<p><font face=\"Times New Roman\" size=\"3\" /></p>\n<p><font face=\"Times New Roman\" size=\"3\">In this environment, I could post a bliki containing multiple media, if necessary.\u00c2\u00a0 The bliki environment then will provide appropriate environment to you and others to help me refine the post (or the product) and also will provide other infrastructure that allows me to link my bliki with other relevant blikis and other people to link to my bliki.\u00c2\u00a0 This environment also allows ability to people to comment or in a way to blog on my bliki and also produce comments and annotations on each component of my material or the material that is added by anybody else.</font></p>\n<p><font face=\"Times New Roman\" size=\"3\" /></p>\n<p><font size=\"3\" /><font face=\"Times New Roman\">It appears that such an environment will encourage building communities around some important events.\u00c2\u00a0 So I attend an event and post a bliki containing my impressions, in audio, visual, and textual form.\u00c2\u00a0\u00c2\u00a0 You see this and add may be photos that you took at the same event ( say wedding of a mutual friend or a conference that we are both interested in or a football game,,,).\u00c2\u00a0 This event bliki starts growing with addition from everybody who may have taken pictures.\u00c2\u00a0 People may blog about this post and may link this event to other related events.\u00c2\u00a0 And this keeps growing.\u00c2\u00a0 </font></p>\n<p><font face=\"Times New Roman\" size=\"3\" /></p>\n<p><font face=\"Times New Roman\" size=\"3\">In a bliki environment you can comment on a post but can also help enrich and refine it actively.</font></p>\n  \t\t\t</div>\r\n  \t\t\t<div class=\"entry-meta-bar clearfix\">\t
