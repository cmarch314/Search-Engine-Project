id=\"content_title\">Thunderbird Setup for ICS Gmail</div>\n\n<blockquote>\n<div id=\"content_text\">\n\n<p><em>This tutorial is based on Thunderbird 24.3.0. If you're using a different version, the naming convention may differ, but the settings will remain the same.</em></p>\n<p>&#160;</p>\n<p>This guide shows how to setup and access your ICS Gmail account via a web browser and/or Thunderbird.</p>\n<h3>Activating your ICS Gmail Account</h3>\n<ol>\n<li>If you have not changed your ICS password recently (since summer 2013), go to&#160;<strong>My Account</strong> at&#160;<a href=\"#elnino.ics.uci.edu\" target=\"_blank\">elnino.ics.uci.edu</a>&#160;to change it. &#160;You may skip Steps 1 and 2 if you already completed them as illustrated <a href=\"google_setup.php\" target=\"_blank\">here</a>.&#160;</li>\n<li>Set your email forwarding to ICS Gmail by selecting <strong>ICS Gmail</strong> at&#160;<a href=\"#elnino.ics.uci.edu\" target=\"_blank\">elnino.ics.uci.edu</a>, too. &#160;Save your new settings. &#160;The changes can take up to two hours to propagate.</li>\n<li>Activate your ICS Gmail by visiting <a href=\"http://www.gmail.com\" target=\"_blank\">www.gmail.com</a>&#160;and entering your ICS credentials (username@ics.uci.edu and your ICS password). &#160;If you already have a personal Gmail account, you will need to click <strong>Add an Account</strong>&#160;to proceed. &#160;If your password was successfully changed, you should be able to log in and will receive a welcome agreement/screen. &#160;Please accept and continue to UCI Gmail's inbox. &#160;If you wish to read your ICS mail only via a web browser, you can stop here. Otherwise, continue. &#160;</li>\n<li>If you will be using&#160;Thunderbird (or any other mail client) to read your ICS Gmail mail, you need to enable Gmail's IMAP feature at&#160;<a href=\"http://www.gmail.com\" target=\"_blank\">www.gmail.com</a>. &#160;Select&#160;the <strong>Gear symbol</strong> on your top right corner, <strong>Settings</strong>,&#160;<strong>Forwarding and POP/IMAP</strong>, <strong>Enable Map</strong>, and&#160;<strong>Save Changes</strong>.</li>\n<li>Now, choose which folders you want to display on your email client. &#160;Select&#160;the&#160;<strong>Gear symbol</strong>&#160;on your top right corner,&#160;<strong>Settings</strong>, <strong>Labels</strong>, and click on&#160;<strong>Show</strong>&#160;and <strong>Show in IMAP </strong>as needed.&#160;</li>\n<li>Next, you will add your newly-created ICS Gmail account to Thunderbird. &#160;</li>\n</ol>\n<h3>Configuring Thunderbird for ICS Gmail</h3>\n<ol>\n<li>Start Thunderbird for the first time or go to <strong>Tools</strong>, <strong>Account Settings</strong>, down to <strong>Account Actions</strong>, select, and&#160;<strong>Add Mail Account</strong> if you have existing accounts configured on Thunderbird.</li>\n<li>In the <b>Mail Account Setup</b> window, enter your name and email address. &#160;Enter your password here if you want to have it remembered. Click <strong>Continue</strong>.</li>\n<li>Thunderbird will now attempt to look for the IMAP information. Please make sure that you have <strong>IMAP </strong>selected.</li>\n<li>Click on <b>Manual config </b>in the lower left corner. &#160;Enter the information as follows:\n<ul>\n<li>Incoming\n<ul>\n<li>IMAP</li>\n<li>Server hostname: <strong>imap.gmail.com</strong></li>\n<li>Port: <strong>993</strong></li>\n<li>SSL: <strong>SSL/TLS</strong></li>\n<li>Authentication: <strong>Normal password</strong></li>\n</ul>\n</li>\n<li>Outgoing\n<ul>\n<li>Server hostname:&#160;<strong>smtp.gmail.com</strong></li>\n<li>Port:&#160;<strong>587</strong></li>\n<li>SSL:&#160;<strong>STARTTLS</strong></li>\n<li>Authentication:&#160;<strong>Normal password</strong></li>\n<li>Username:<strong>&#160;username@ics.uci.edu</strong></li>\n</ul>\n</li>\n<p>&#160;</p>\n<img alt=\"server configuration\" height=\"431\" hspace=\"NaN\" src=\"../bin/img/email/servers\" style=\"display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;\" width=\"751\"/>\n<p>&#160;</p>\n<li>Click <strong>Re-test</strong> and then <strong>Done</strong>.</li>\n<li>If you <em>did not</em> choose to have your password saved, you will now be prompted to enter your email password.\n<ul>\n</ul>\n</li>\n</ul>\n</li>\n<li>Go to <strong>Tools </strong>and select <strong>Account Settings</strong>. Under your new account's&#160;<strong>Server Settings</strong>, click on the <b>Advanced</b>&#160;button to your right.\n<ul>\n<li>Make sure that <b>IMAP server directory</b> is empty.&#160;</li>\n<li>Uncheck <b>Show only subscribed folders</b> if checked.&#160;</li>\n<li>Uncheck <b>Allow server to override these namespaces</b>&#160;if checked.</li>\n</ul>\n</li>\n<p>&#160;</p>\n<img alt=\"Additional steps\" height=\"345\" src=\"../bin/img/email/advanced.jpg\" width=\"397\"/>\n<p>&#160;</p>\n<li>If you have multiple accounts, you might want to display your ICS Gmail account first. &#160;To do so, on&#160;<strong>Account Settings</strong>, select&#160;your ICS Gmail account from the left pane,&#160;<strong>Account Actions</strong>&#160;at the bottom,&#160;<strong>Set as Default</strong>.&#160; Please restart to have the changes applied.</li>\n<li>If you want multiple accounts and want to use ICS Gmail to send emails, on&#160;<strong>Account Settings</strong>, select&#160;<strong>Outgoing Server (SMTP)</strong>&#160;at the bottom of the left pane. &#160;Select&#160;<strong>smtp.gmail.com</strong>&#160;and click on&#160;<strong>Set Default</strong>. &#160;Make sure that your SMTP username is listed as username@ics.uci.edu. &#160;Click&#160;<strong>OK</strong>. &#160;\n<ul>\n</ul>\n</li>\n<li>You may copy signatures between accounts. &#160;On&#160;<strong>Account Settings</strong>, select the account you want to copy the signature from on&#160;the left pane, go to <strong>Signature text</strong> on the right and copy whatever text/file path you have set there. &#160;Now select the account you want to copy the signature to and paste whatever text/file path you copied.</li>\n<li>This last step is optional. If you have many email messages, you may want to disable Message Synchronizing so that your email loads more quickly.&#160;\n<ul>\n<li>On <strong>Account Settings</strong>, select <strong>Synchronization &amp; Storage</strong> for your new account. &#160;</li>\n<li>Uncheck <strong>Keep messages for this account on this computer</strong>.</li>\n</ul>\n</li>\n</ol>\nNow you may start drag and dropping messages and folders within your accounts. &#160;Please note that if your folders contain thousands of messages, it will take a long time to complete the transfer. &#160;Plan accordingly. &#160;If you have any questions, email helpdesk@ics.uci.edu.\n<ul>\n</ul>\n\n\n</div>\n</blockquote>\n</div>\n<div id=\"sidebar\"></div>\n<div id=\"footer\"><a href=\"http://www.uci.edu/copyright.php\">Copyright Inquiries</a> |\n   <a href=\"http://www.uci.edu/cgi-bin/phonebook\">UCI Directory</a> |\n   <a href=\"http://intranet.ics.uci.edu/\">Intranet</a> |\n   \n   <!-- <a href=\"http://www.ics.uci.edu/sitemap.php\">Site Map</a> | -->\n \n  <a href=\"mailto: i%63%73%77%65%62m%61s%74%65r@%69c%73%2e%75c%69%2eedu\">icswebmaster</a> |\nUpdated: \nSeptember 12 2014</div>\n</div>\n<script type=\"text/javascript\">\nvar gaJsHost = ((\"https:\" == document.location.protocol) ? \"https://ssl.\" : \"http://www.\");\ndocument.write(unescape(\"%3Cscript src='\" + gaJsHost + \"google-analytics.com/ga.js' type='text/javascript'%3E%3C/script%3E\"));\n</script>\n<script type=\"text/javascript\">\nvar pageTracker = _gat._getTracker(\"UA-605898-1\");\npageTracker._trackPageview();\n</script>\n</body>\n</html>", "id": 7577.0}
