The NSF GRF program recognizes and supports outstanding graduate students who have demonstrated their potential for significant achievements in science and engineering research. Fellows receive three years of support, including a $30,000 annual stipend.\n</i>\n</p>\n<!-- -->\n<!-- -->\n<p class=\"image_with_wrapped_text\">\n<img alt=\"photo: Kevin Bache\" class=\"left\" height=\"200\" src=\"../../../bin/img/photos/spotlight/Spotlight-KevinBache.jpg\" width=\"141\"/>\n</p>\n<p>\n<b>Where are you from?</b>\n<br/>\nI grew up in Poland, Ohio, and Santa Fe, N.M.\n</p>\n<p>\n<b>What are your previous academic degrees?</b>  \n<br/>\nA.B. in Social and Cognitive Neuroscience from Harvard College.\n</p>\n<p>\n<b>Why did you choose to pursue graduate studies at UCI?</b>\n<br/>\nUCI has fantastic machine learning.  The weather isn't too bad either.\n</p>\n<p>\n<b>What is your current research focus? Why did you choose to go into this field of study?</b>\n<br/>\nDoing neuroimaging research after undergrad, I found myself increasingly frustrated with the limits of the field&#8217;s mathematical tools. I started digging deeper into the imaging methods literature and was consistently impressed by the work coming from machine learning groups. I started learning all I could about the field and two years later, here I am. My current research is still in flux, but it&#8217;s rooted in developing probabilistic modeling tools to explore complex datasets.\n</p>\n<p>\n<b>What are your future plans?</b> \n<br/>\nI'd be happy anywhere that has vibrant people and big, difficult problems.\n</p>\n<p>\n<b>Any tips for fellow students who may want to apply for the NSF GRFP?</b>\n<br/>\nThe reviewers don&#8217;t have much time to spend on you.  Make sure your essays present a clear, digestible narrative that explains why you&#8217;d make a good candidate. And spend more energy on the broader impacts than you think you need to.\n</p>\n<p>\n<b>How do you like to spend your time outside of school?</b>\n<br/>\nI have fantastic group of friends here at UCI, with whom I spend most of my free time. I also love to cook, climb, hike and spend time in the woods.\n</p>\n\n\n</div>\n</blockquote>\n</div>\n<div id=\"sidebar\"><div id=\"sidebar_title\">More community &#187;</div>\n<ul>\n<li><a href=\"../../alumni/index.php\">Alumni</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"../../friends/index.php\">Friends</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"http://www.parents.uci.edu/\">Parents</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"../../friends/leadershipcouncil/index.php\">Leadership Council</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"../index.php\">News</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"https://ua-web.uadv.uci.edu/eGiving/\">Give a gift</a></li>\n</ul></div>\n<div id=\"footer\"><a href=\"http://www.uci.edu/copyright.php\">Copyright Inquiries</a> |\n   <a href=\"http://www.uci.edu/cgi-bin/phonebook\">UCI Directory</a> |\n   <a href=\"http://intranet.ics.uci.edu/\">Intranet</a> |\n   \n   <!-- <a href=\"http://www.ics.uci.edu/sitemap.php\">Site Map</a> | -->\n \n  <a href=\"mailto: i%63%73%77%65%62m%61s%74%65r@%69c%73%2e%75c%69%2eedu\">icswebmaster</a></div>\n</div>\n<script type=\"text/javascript\">\nvar gaJsHost = ((\"https:\" == document.location.protocol) ? \"https://ssl.\" : \"http://www.\");\ndocument.write(unescape(\"%3Cscript src='\" + gaJsHost + \"google-analytics.com/ga.js' type='text/javascript'%3E%3C/script%3E\"));\n</script>\n<script type=\"text/javascript\">\nvar pageTracker = _gat._getTracker(\"UA-605898-1\");\npageTracker._trackPageview();\n</script>\n</body>\n</html>", "id": 16650.0}
