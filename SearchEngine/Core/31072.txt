is a database consisting of instances of the Chimera\nhypermedia concepts and their associated attributes. Hyperwebs are stored\nunder a WWW server's namespace and thus have an associated URL. All hyperwebs\non a particular website share the same set of viewers, objects, and views.\nWhat varies between hyperwebs is the set of view-specific anchors and links.\nHyperwebs can be composed into hierarchies. This allows hyperwebs lower\nin the hierarchy to inherit the anchors and links found in its parents.\nNote, Chimera does not implement true inheritance however, since there is\nno notion of an anchor or link in a subweb overriding the definition of\nan anchor or link found in one of its ancestors. Only single inheritance\nis allowed.
