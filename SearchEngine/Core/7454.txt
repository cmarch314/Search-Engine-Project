Program Overview</div>\n\n<blockquote>\n<div id=\"content_text\">\n\n<p>The Bren School offers M.S. degrees in Computer Science, Information\nand Computer Science, Software Engineering, Networked Systems, and Statistics.</p>\n<p>The objective of the terminal M.S. degree is to prepare students for a\nvariety of advanced careers in the computing industry (e.g., software\ndevelopment, information technology consulting, application specific integrated\ncircuit design, network design and configuration, embedded systems design,\nalgorithms development, data mining). The Master's program can be pursued full-\nor part-time, and required courses may be taken during the first and second year of study. It should take no longer than two years if pursued full-time.</p>\n<hr style=\"width: 100%;\" width=\"100%\"/>\n<p><strong><span style=\"color: #6699cc;\"><strong>&#187;</strong></span> M.S. Exams and Reviews</strong></p>\n<p><span style=\"text-decoration: underline;\">Graduate Student Review</span></p>\n<p>At the end of each spring quarter, a\nformal review is conducted of all M.S. students. If unsatisfactory progress is being made, a\nletter of probation will be sent to the student and placed in their file. The\nprobation period varies, depending on each student's academic situation. If the\nproblem is not cleared during the probation period, the ICS Graduate Office may\nrecommend dismissal from the program.\nIt is the student's responsibility to make sure the ICS Graduate Office is\naware of any problem that has been cleared during the probation period. </p>\n<p><span style=\"text-decoration: underline;\">Advancing to Candidacy for the M.S. Degree</span></p>\n<p>Advancement to candidacy implies that a student is almost finished with\nhis/her degree requirements. This is not an automatic process. The students is\nresponsible for making sure his or her form is completed and gets filed. The\nAdvancement to Candidacy should be submitted at least one quarter before the\nstudent plans on graduating. </p>\n<p><span style=\"text-decoration: underline;\">Comprehensive Exam (Option 1)</span></p>\n<p>Students who elect for the Comprehensive Exam option must pass a\ncomprehensive examination offered by the department or by a representative of\nthe specific research area. Students should speak to the ICS Graduate Counselor\nor research area representative regarding test dates, preparation, and reading\nlists. The faculty within the particular area develops the exam; this may\nresult in the content and structure varying from year to year.</p>\n<p><span style=\"text-decoration: underline;\">Thesis Option (Option 2)</span></p>\n<p>The thesis option is available to all Bren School M.S. students, except for those in the Statistics Program, who do not plan to take\nthe comprehensive exam and who are in good academic standing with the School.\nStudents pursuing this option must enroll in at least two quarters of Thesis\nSupervision, which will substitute for two required courses\nas specified under the concentration area or track of choice. A committee will guide the student and give final approval of the\nthesis. An oral presentation of the thesis to the committee is required.</p>\n<p>&#160;</p>\n<p><strong><span style=\"color: #6699cc;\"><strong>&#187;</strong></span> Degree Conferral</strong></p>\nThe M.S. degree will be conferred by the UCI Graduate Division the quarter\nfollowing advancement to candidacy if all degree requirements are satisfied.\nGraduate Division will send a letter of degree confirmation. Diplomas will be\nready for pick up from the Registrar's Office about four months after\ngraduation.\n\n\n</div>\n</blockquote>\n</div>\n<div id=\"sidebar\"><div id=\"sidebar_title\">More Graduate &#187;</div>\n<ul>\n<li><a href=\"../admissions/index.php\">Prospective students</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"index.php\">Programs of study</a>\n<ul>\n<li><a href=\"degrees_MSOverview.php\">M.S. degree overview</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"degrees_PhDOverview.php\">Ph.D. degree overview</a></li>\n</ul>\n</li>\n<li><a href=\"../courses/index.php\">Academic year plan</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"../forms/index.php\">Forms</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"../policies/index.php\">Policies</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"../funding/index.php\">Funding &amp; housing</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"../../../computing/account/new.php\">Computing support</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"../resources.php\">Campus resources</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"../../about/visit/index.php\">Visit the Bren School</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"../../bin/pdf/grad/Graduate Student Handbook F14-15.pdf\">Graduate Student Handbook</a></li>\n</ul></div>\n<div id=\"footer\"><a href=\"http://www.uci.edu/copyright.php\">Copyright Inquiries</a> |\n   <a href=\"http://www.uci.edu/cgi-bin/phonebook\">UCI Directory</a> |\n   <a href=\"http://intranet.ics.uci.edu/\">Intranet</a> |\n   \n   <!-- <a href=\"http://www.ics.uci.edu/sitemap.php\">Site Map</a> | -->\n \n  <a href=\"mailto: i%63%73%77%65%62m%61s%74%65r@%69c%73%2e%75c%69%2eedu\">icswebmaster</a></div>\n</div>\n<script type=\"text/javascript\">\nvar gaJsHost = ((\"https:\" == document.location.protocol) ? \"https://ssl.\" : \"http://www.\");\ndocument.write(unescape(\"%3Cscript src='\" + gaJsHost + \"google-analytics.com/ga.js' type='text/javascript'%3E%3C/script%3E\"));\n</script>\n<script type=\"text/javascript\">\nvar pageTracker = _gat._getTracker(\"UA-605898-1\");\npageTracker._trackPageview();\n</script>\n</body>\n</html>", "id": 7454.0}
