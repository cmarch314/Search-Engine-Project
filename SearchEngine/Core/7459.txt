Academic standing is determined by GPA and degree progress. To stay in good standing:</p>\n<ul>\n<li>Maintain a quarterly and cumulative\nGPA above 2.0, and</li>\n<li>Make progress towards your chosen\ndegree</li>\n</ul>\n<p>&#160;</p>\n<hr style=\"width: 100%;\" width=\"100%\"/>\n<p>&#160;</p>\n<p><strong><span style=\"color: #6699cc;\"><strong>&#187; </strong></span>Academic\nProbation</strong></p>\n<p>You are subject to probation any time\nyour quarterly or cumulative GPA falls below 2.0.</p>\n<p>The Student Affairs Office will notify you\nof your probation status by email to your UCI account.&#160; Certain conditions that must be met to return to good academic standing will be specified in a \"contract.\" It outlines specific academic progress expectations and a timeframe by which\nyou must return to good academic standing.</p>\n<p>Contact your Bren School academic\ncounselor soon after your probation notification to ensure that your academic\nplan is reasonable and to learn about campus resources and success strategies\nthat may be helpful.</p>\n<p>It is your responsibility to manage your\nUCI account and to read and respond to official messages from your academic\ncounselors in a timely manner. Failure to follow through on the steps outlined\nin your probation letter may result in an administrative hold being placed on\nyour record.</p>\n<p>&#160;</p>\n<p><span style=\"color: #6699cc;\">&#160;</span><strong>Disqualification</strong></p>\n<p>This is a preliminary step in the review and decision-making process;\nit is <strong>not</strong> the same as being \"formally disqualified\". Subject to disqualification is a result of failing to meet the conditions of an academic contract after TWO consecutive quarters.</p>\n<p>Official notification will be sent from the Bren School's Associate Dean for Student Affairs to the student's UCI email address. The notification letter provides\ninstructions for appealing the disqualification action.\n</p>\n<p>All students are <b>strongly</b> encouraged to appeal the disqualification notice by submitting an appeal\nletter to the Associate Dean. The letter can be emailed to <a href=\"mailto:adsa@ics.uci.edu\" target=\"_blank\" title=\"Associate Dean's email\">adsa@ics.uci.edu</a>.</p>\n<p>&#160;</p>\n<p><strong><span style=\"color: #6699cc;\"><strong>&#187; </strong></span>Submitting a Letter of Appeal</strong></p>\n<p>A letter of appeal should explain any\nunusual or extenuating circumstances that contributed to the student's academic\ndifficulties.&#160; It must also detail the student's plans and actions for effectively\naddressing these circumstances (including the use of campus resources). </p>\n<p>The Associate Dean will carefully review\nthe student's appeal letter, transcript and academic file with the counselors\nbefore making a decision. Note\nthat the Associate Dean for Student Affairs is the chief academic officer for\nthe Bren School of ICS, and his decisions regarding disqualification appeals\nare <strong>final</strong>.</p>\n<p>If the appeal is granted, the student\nwill be held to an academic contract that outlines the expectations and\ntimeframe for returning to good academic standing. </p>\n<p>&#160;</p>\n<p>If the appeal is not granted, the\nstudent will be formally disqualified by the end of the current quarter. Formal disqualification means dismissal from the major, the\nBren School of ICS, and UC Irvine. The student will be restricted from registering for courses\nor using university services after a stated date. The Registrar's Office will also be\ninstructed to note the effective date of the disqualification on the student's\nofficial transcript.An official letter will be sent to the student's permanent address on file.</p>\n<p><strong><span style=\"color: #6699cc;\"><strong>&#187; </strong></span>Readmission</strong></p>\n<p>If a student has been formally disqualified, the student is encouraged to make an appointment to speak with an academic counselor to learn about the readmission policy and to strategize future plans and goals. Please visit the page on <a href=\"Withdrawal_Readmission.php\" target=\"_blank\" title=\"Withdrawal and Readmission\">Withdrawal and Readmission</a> for additional information.</p>\n\n\n</div>\n</blockquote>\n</div>\n<div id=\"sidebar\"><div id=\"sidebar_title\">More Undergraduate &#187;</div>\n<ul>\n<li><a href=\"../sao/index.php\">Academic advising</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"../courses/index.php\">Academic year plan</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"../degrees/index.php\">ICS majors</a>\n<ul>\n<li><a href=\"../degrees/Double_Majors.php\">Double major</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"../degrees/Second_BA.php\">2nd baccalaureate</a></li>\n</ul>\n</li>\n<li><a href=\"../degrees/Minors.php\">ICS minors</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"../QA_Petitions.php\">Petitions</a></li>\n<li><span style=\"font-family: Verdana,Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif;
