\t\t\t\tEventWeb 12: A scenario &#8211; Conferences    \t\t\t</h1><!-- .entry-title -->\r\n  \t\t\t</header>\r\n\r\n  \t\t\t\r\n  \t\t\t\r\n  \t\t\t<div class=\"entry-content clearfix\">\r\n    \t\t\t<p><font face=\"Times New Roman\" size=\"3\">Examples usually help in illustrating concepts.\u00c2\u00a0 So here is an example from a familiar area of conferences.\u00c2\u00a0 Obviously, this could be easily extended to other things like sports, meeting, seminars, weddings etc.\u00c2\u00a0 In this and some other posts, however, it will help to focus on this application scenario.</font></p>\n<p><font face=\"Times New Roman\" size=\"3\">Conferences are major events to build strong communities.\u00c2\u00a0 Conferences are planned and organized well in advance.\u00c2\u00a0 People who are part of the community start planning to attend conferences, they contribute to different mail lists, now \u00e2\u20ac\u02dcfeeds\u00e2\u20ac\u2122, to receive information.\u00c2\u00a0 They contribute to the organization of conferences in many ways ranging from organizers to authors to participants.\u00c2\u00a0 People who want to be in a particular area, say Multimedia, they find out important conference events in that area and plan to attend.</font></p>\n<p><font size=\"3\"></font><font face=\"Times New Roman\">Then conference takes place.\u00c2\u00a0 There are many events at the conference.\u00c2\u00a0 Papers are presented in many sessions.\u00c2\u00a0 Demos and posters are presented in others.\u00c2\u00a0 With some conferences there are exhibits.\u00c2\u00a0 Reception, business meetings, banquets, and social programs are as much a part of the conference as the main conference.\u00c2\u00a0 </font><font size=\"3\"></font><font face=\"Times New Roman\" size=\"3\">Most conferences usually publish a \u00e2\u20ac\u02dcproceedings\u00e2\u20ac\u2122 of the conference.\u00c2\u00a0 This is a slightly misleading term.\u00c2\u00a0 Proceedings are prepared based on the intent of the presentation.\u00c2\u00a0 They are not the presentations made by people and they don\u00e2\u20ac\u2122t capture discussions.\u00c2\u00a0 They also are just text, while the real value of the conference is in the interactions and experiences.</font></p>\n<p><font face=\"Times New Roman\" size=\"3\">Now suppose that we create each paper, each demo, each social function, and any other event at the conference as an event.\u00c2\u00a0 We collect text-based information such as papers, presentations (say powerpoints) photos and videos of what happens in that event.\u00c2\u00a0 We post all these things as an Event (I will use Event to denote computer representation of all the material related to an event).\u00c2\u00a0 After the event, people can post photos and videos that they may have taken related to the event.\u00c2\u00a0 People can also comment on the event and have discussions going.</font></p>\n<p><font face=\"Times New Roman\" size=\"3\">A paper presented at a conference may have relationship with other paper at that conference.\u00c2\u00a0 It may also have relationship other papers presented at other conferences and published in journals.\u00c2\u00a0 It may be related to some product launches or other events taking place at other place.\u00c2\u00a0 Multiple papers may have relationships among them based on authors, topics, organizations, or any other aspect.\u00c2\u00a0 A paper may result in spawning new fields, killing an existing field, development of a product, tenure for a researcher, or even friendship between two researchers.</font></p>\n<p><font face=\"Times New Roman\" size=\"3\">Conferences are related to each other in many ways also.\u00c2\u00a0 ACM as a professional organization is involved in making sure that all aspects of computing progress.\u00c2\u00a0 It encourages organization of many conferences and workshops.\u00c2\u00a0 These events are related to each other in many different ways.\u00c2\u00a0 There are conference series \u00e2\u20ac\u201c ACM Multimedia takes place every year.\u00c2\u00a0 There are workshops and tutorials that take place in conjunction to a conference.\u00c2\u00a0 As research fields evolve, common topics are addressed.\u00c2\u00a0 The relationships among such events (conferences or workshops) can be judged by sub-events (papers and their topics), and also by participants.\u00c2\u00a0 If the papers at two conferences are from similar topic areas and the people involved are similar then one knows that the two events are similar.</font></p>\n<p><font face=\"Times New Roman\" size=\"3\">One judges the prominence of people in a field by analyzing their role in these events.\u00c2\u00a0 Similarly the strength of a field is judged by how these events are changing over time and space.\u00c2\u00a0 For example, there are many conferences starting in Multimedia related area in Asian countries reflecting the growth of this field in Asia more than Europe.</font></p>\n<p><font face=\"Times New Roman\" size=\"3\">As may be clear, there are many different relationships among these events and can be captured by creating Events on them.</font></p>\n<p><font face=\"Times New Roman\" size=\"3\">To give an idea of how some of these will grow, I decided to use some conferences in Multimedia as examples.\u00c2\u00a0 I am creating some of these Events in Seraja. (Disclosure: I am Chairman of Seraja)\u00c2\u00a0 I will use ACM Multimedia 2006 conference as an example.</font></p>\n<p><font size=\"3\"></font><font face=\"Times New Roman\">Here is an Event in Seraja.\u00c2\u00a0 An interesting way to look at Seraja is that it is an environment to start with an \u00e2\u20ac\u02dcevent blog\u00e2\u20ac\u2122 that becomes \u00e2\u20ac\u02dcEvent Wiki\u00e2\u20ac\u2122.\u00c2\u00a0 In this environment, an event could become richer and richer when people start adding to it.\u00c2\u00a0 So if to the ACMMM2006 event that I created other people could start adding other photos and reports and even could create other papers by posting their presentations as an independent but related event, then the whole event could be stored and used by attendees as well as other people.\u00c2\u00a0 As an example, I also created several other events related to ACMMM2006 \u00e2\u20ac\u201c a few papers etc, biz meeting, banquet etc to give examples.\u00c2\u00a0 Using the tools that will come in a month or so, we will be able to relate (by creating explicit and exploring implicit links) events and start creating a web.\u00c2\u00a0 We could start linking related papers and conferences and track the progress of the individual research project, research area, and the whole fields using the EventWeb that will be created.\u00c2\u00a0 I believe that this would be an interesting way to capture conference proceedings \u00e2\u20ac\u201c including the live webcasts \u00e2\u20ac\u201c rather than current outmoded version of \u00e2\u20ac\u02dcfrozen\u00e2\u20ac\u2122 proceedings.\u00c2\u00a0 </font><font size=\"3\"></font><font face=\"Times New Roman\" size=\"3\">And we have not even started considering all the other personal, travel, business, and entertainment events that usually take place around a conference.</font></p>\n<p><font face=\"Times New Roman\" size=\"3\" /><font face=\"Times New Roman\" size=\"3\"><font face=\"Times New Roman\" size=\"3\" /></p>\n<p></font></p>\n  \t\t\t</div>\r\n  \t\t\t<div class=\"entry-meta-bar clearfix\">\t        \t\t\t\r\n    \t\t\t<div
