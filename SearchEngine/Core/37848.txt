Reactions</A>\n</H1>\n\n<P>\nReaction processing in OEChem is divided into two categories:\nunimolecular reactions and library generation.  Unimolecular reactions\nare useful for (although not limited to) normalization reactions. The\n<code>OEUniMolecularRxn</code> class in OEChem applies chemical\ntransformations to individual molecules.  Reactions can also be used\nto generate combinatorial libraries using OEChem.  Both 'clipping' and\nreaction based enumeration can be achieved using the\n<code>OELibraryGen</code> class.\n\n<P>\nReactions are represented in OEChem as query molecules\n(<code>OEQMol</code>).  Sets of chemical transform operations are derived\nfrom reaction molecules by differences between the reactant and\nproduct patterns and in the reaction molecule.  For example, atoms and\nbonds that appear in the reactant pattern, but are absent the in the\nproduct pattern are 'destroyed'.  Atoms and bonds that appear in the\nproduct pattern but not in the reactant pattern are 'created'.  Atoms\nare tracked between reactants and products by means atom maps.  Atom\nmaps are stored and retrieved using the <code>SetMapIdx</code> and\n<code>GetMapIdx</code> methods.  Product atoms that have the same map index\nas reactant atoms originate from their reactant counterpart.\nReactions are completely defined by a fields in a query molecule.\nReaction molecules can be created from virtually any reaction file\nformat (SMIRKS, MDL RXN, etc.), and can even be constructed\nprogrammatically.\n\n<P>\n\n<p><hr>\n<!--Table of Child-Links-->\n<A NAME=\"CHILD_LINKS\"><STRONG>Subsections</STRONG></a>\n\n<UL CLASS=\"ChildLinks\">\n<LI><A href=\"node249.html\">28.1 Normalization Reactions</a>\n<LI><A href=\"node250.html\">28.2 Library Generation</a>\n</ul>\n<!--End of Table of Child-Links-->\n\n<DIV CLASS=\"navigation\">\n<p><hr>\n<table align=\"center\" border=\"0\" width=\"100%\" cellpadding=\"0\" cellspacing=\"0\">\n<tr>\n<td rowspan=2><A HREF=\"node247.html\"><img src=\"../icons/previous.gif\"\n  border=\"0\" alt=\"Previous Page\"></A></td>\n<td rowspan=2><A HREF=\"pyprog.html\"><img src=\"../icons/up.gif\"\n  border=\"0\" alt=\"Up One Level\"></A></td>\n<td rowspan=2><A HREF=\"node249.html\"><img src=\"../icons/next.gif\"\n  border=\"0\" alt=\"Next Page\"></A></td><td width=\"100%\" rowspan=\"2\"> </td>\n<td valign=\"center\" align=\"right\" height=\"56\" background=\"../icons/title_bkgd.gif\" colspan=4 class=\"navigation\">OEChem -
