Are</span><h2>About</h2></div>\n<div class=\"eight columns\">\n<div class=\"heading\"><h5> <strong>What do we do at EVOKE Lab &amp; Studio?</strong>\u00a0</h5></div>\n<div class=\"custom-padding-box clearfix\" style=\"padding:0px 5px 0px 0px;\">\n<p>We start with an eye toward social issues and concerns, and integrate social theory, art, computer science, and design to create new technologies and artifacts that evoke thought, debate, discussion, and consideration of a more harmonious mediated world. Our projects range from small experiments with new innovations to large-scale studies on major issues like &#8220;big data&#8221;, privacy, social justice, and self-exploration through technology. We are an open community of interdisciplinary scholars and designers who have fun, learn, and grow together, and are always looking for more fellow travelers!</p>\n</div>\n</div>\n<div class=\"eight columns\">\n<div class=\"heading\"><h5> <strong>Where do we work?</strong> </h5></div>\n<div class=\"custom-padding-box clearfix\" style=\"padding:0px 0px 0px 5px;\">\n<p>We are located at the UC-Irvine campus in the Calit2 Building. While our research work takes us everywhere, and our field sites are varied and far-flung, much of our work takes place in the EVOKE Lab &amp; Studio itself. Our Design Lab is modeled after the workflow of the User Experience (UX) design process, taking ideas from inception to formal design specs. When it&#8217;s time to start building, projects shift into the larger, adjacent Studio space where we move from idea to physical reality. See below for more details on what we have in the Lab &amp; Studio.</p>\n</div>\n</div>\n<p>&nbsp;</p>\n<div class=\"one-third column\">\n<p><a title=\"The EVOKE Lab\" href=\"http://evoke.ics.uci.edu/newsite/?page_id=229\"><div class=\"info\"><img src=\"http://evoke.ics.uci.edu/newsite/wp-content/uploads/EVOKE-Lab-Icon.png\" alt=\"\" class=\"scale-with-grid\"><h5>The EVOKE Lab</h5></a></p>\n<p>Where the creative work happens, the Lab is set up to support UX design workflow from blue-sky sessions to conceptual prototyping.<br />\n<a title=\"The EVOKE Lab\" href=\"http://evoke.ics.uci.edu/newsite/?page_id=229\">Find out more about what&#8217;s in the EVOKE Lab.</a></p>\n</div>\n</div>\n<div class=\"one-third column\">\n<p><a title=\"Values in Design\" href=\"http://evoke.ics.uci.edu/newsite/?page_id=219\"><div class=\"info\"><img src=\"http://evoke.ics.uci.edu/newsite/wp-content/uploads/VID-Icon.png\" alt=\"\" class=\"scale-with-grid\"><h5>Values in Design</h5></a></p>\n<p>How do technologies reflect our human values? How do we design social values into our technologies and systems?
