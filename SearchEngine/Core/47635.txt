some hectic activity among flight staff and call for a doctor was related to a child birth at 38,000 ft in air.\u00c2\u00a0 The little boy had difficulty breathing and the doctor on board &#8212; a passenger &#8212; helped him survive and both mother and the baby were taken to hospital in Charlotte.\u00c2\u00a0 Ambulance was waiting when we landed.</p>\n<p>Interesting flight.\u00c2\u00a0 Of course, the price of this excitement was a missed connection in Atlanta and rerouting me to Los Angeles.\u00c2\u00a0 But that is a very small price to pay to be on the flight where such excitement took place.\u00c2\u00a0 It was reassuring to see how the staff reacted and how all the preparations were in place when the plane landed in Charlotte.\u00c2\u00a0 It seems both the mother and the child
