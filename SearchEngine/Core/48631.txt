We&#8217;re really very excited to bring something we think is entirely different to the (already very noisy) world of search.</p>\n<p>As always, though, the proof of the pudding is in the tasting and that&#8217;s why I was really happy to see that, within a couple of hours of use, you found your way to a link you wouldn&#8217;t have thought of searching for or found through normal keyword search.  That&#8217;s exactly what Pico is all about.</p>\n<p>Keyword search is important and works well &#8230; but only for certain tasks.  Every time you interact with a piece of content or information on your computer, there are hundreds of related pieces of content out there.  Normally, that web of related stuff is hidden &#8230; you have to know where to get it or decide to do an explicit search to uncover it.  Pico works by constantly reading what you&#8217;re reading and using its understanding of that to expose that web of related stuff &#8230; we call that web the Latent Web; Pico exposes the Latent Web.</p>\n<p>Anyway, enough proselytizing for one post!
