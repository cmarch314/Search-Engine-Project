experience.</p>\n<p>Mr Chopra is definitely a very different director than others.\u00c2\u00a0 This movie is produced and directed so well that while watching the movie, I never thought that it was a Bollywood production &#8212; even though I new it is.\u00c2\u00a0 Movie is directed, filmed, and produced very professionally.\u00c2\u00a0 It does not devite from the theme and carefully presents the new message about &#8216;dharma&#8217;.</p>\n<p>One of the most interesting thing that Vidhu Vinod Chopra said during interview that he makes movies to satisfy himself.\u00c2\u00a0 He wants to be happy that he made the best that he could.\u00c2\u00a0 Another interesting message that I got wa that he uses the basic model of entrepreneurship in his movies.\u00c2\u00a0 He rewards people based on the success of the movie, rather than contracting them.\u00c2\u00a0 He claimed that this results in allowing him to make movies at reasonable cost.\u00c2\u00a0 And there are good actors who are willing to work under that model.</p>\n<p>I hope that other movie makers in India learn somethings from him and make moview that are in that class.\u00c2\u00a0 He is well aware of the problem.\u00c2\u00a0 He mentioned that Indian audience has not appreciated that movie.\u00c2\u00a0 In fact I was told that many Indians in USA also have made statements like who wants to see a movie in which Amitabh Bachchan plays the role of a security man.\u00c2\u00a0 Maybe the taste of Bollywood people has become so bad that it will take long time for them to get out of total melodrama and overacting in a very poorly produced environment.\u00c2\u00a0 But then everybody has right to enjoy what art they like.</p>\n<p>For me, yesterday evening was one of the best evening that had a
