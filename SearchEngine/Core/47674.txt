\t\t\t\tSmartWeb presentation at ACMMM07    \t\t\t</h1><!-- .entry-title -->\r\n  \t\t\t</header>\r\n\r\n  \t\t\t\r\n  \t\t\t\r\n  \t\t\t<div class=\"entry-content clearfix\">\r\n    \t\t\t<p><font face=\"Times New Roman\" size=\"3\">Prof. Wolfgang Wahlster gave a keynote talk at ACM MM 2007 on his project on Mobile SmartWeb.\u00c2\u00a0 In this project he is developing exciting technology for vehicles to access Web in multimodal format.\u00c2\u00a0 This system used multimodal input and multimodal output on mobile devices.\u00c2\u00a0 It combine most existing approaches in Ontology, Web search, document analysis and related areas.\u00c2\u00a0 Definitely one of the most interesting project that takes existing and emerging technology from many different areas and combines them intelligently to solve some common problems in efficient and interesting way.</font></p>\n<p><font face=\"Times New Roman\" size=\"3\" /></p>\n<p><font face=\"Times New Roman\" size=\"3\">You can ask a question on &#8212; say &#8212; who was the football champion in 1956 and the system will answer in text (name), photo (picture of the team), video (winning goal), or audio (commentary about the final goal) format.\u00c2\u00a0 Use may request the form as in most search engines.\u00c2\u00a0 The beauty of the project is in integrating different techniques intelligently and providing a compelling mobile environment.\u00c2\u00a0 It also takes the car\u00e2\u20ac\u2122s information from different sensors to provide all information to the driver.\u00c2\u00a0 It can warn using multimodal methods about potential warnings related to weather and road conditions.\u00c2\u00a0 It uses different modes to sense these conditions and informs the driver using \u00e2\u20ac\u02dccontextual intelligence\u00e2\u20ac\u2122 to minimize cognitive load on the driver.</font></p>\n<p><font face=\"Times New Roman\" size=\"3\">This is a major project &#8212; with budget of about 200 Million Euros.</font></p>\n<p><font face=\"Times New Roman\" size=\"3\">He also brought in a motorcycle and a car to show his working system.</font></p>\n<p>(By the way, I discovered in discussions with him after his talk, that we used be colleagues in 1977 at Universtaet Hamburg\u00c2\u00a0 and used to share our ideas related to traffic videos.)</p>\n  \t\t\t</div>\r\n
