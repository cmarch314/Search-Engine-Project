However, we have taken a design\nphilosophy that molecule objects are primarily data containers with data access\nmember functions.  Most powerful data analysis and manipulation routines in\nOEChem are implemented as free functions rather than member functions.  This\ndecision is based on the realization that the abstraction of a molecule\ncan be neither stable nor consistent.  To one programmer, a molecule should\ndescribe and perceive the space group of organometallic complexes, while to\nanother a molecule should describe the residues and secondary structure of\na macromolecule.  Both of these perspectives are reasonable and should be\nsupported.  Further, a nearly infinite additional list of molecule designs\ncan be imagined and should be supported.  In order to do this, the OEChem\nmolecule must be extensible, light-weights, and easily re-implemented.\nThus, can make major changes to the OEChem\nmolecules, without needed to re-implement more than a handful of functions.\nConversely, we can add an entire new area of chemistry to the OEChem\nrepertoire through free-functions  without needing to implement the\nfunction in multiple molecules.\nWe\nhave used namespaces extensively to keep the free-functions from cluttering the global\nnamespace.\n\n<P>\n\n<DIV CLASS=\"navigation\">\n<p><hr>\n<table align=\"center\" border=\"0\" width=\"100%\" cellpadding=\"0\" cellspacing=\"0\">\n<tr>\n<td rowspan=2><A HREF=\"node270.html\"><img src=\"../icons/previous.gif\"\n  border=\"0\" alt=\"Previous Page\"></A></td>\n<td rowspan=2><A HREF=\"node269.html\"><img src=\"../icons/up.gif\"\n  border=\"0\" alt=\"Up One Level\"></A></td>\n<td rowspan=2><A HREF=\"node272.html\"><img src=\"../icons/next.gif\"\n  border=\"0\" alt=\"Next Page\"></A></td><td width=\"100%\" rowspan=\"2\"> </td>\n<td valign=\"center\" align=\"right\" height=\"56\" background=\"../icons/title_bkgd.gif\" colspan=4 class=\"navigation\">OEChem - C++ Theory Manual\n<BR>\nVersion 1.3.1</td></tr>\n<tr><td><img src=\"../icons/spacer_1.gif\"\n  border=\"0\" alt=\"\"></td>\n<td><A href=\"contents.html\"><img src=\"../icons/contents.gif\"\n  border=\"0\" alt=\"Contents\"></A></td>\n<td><img src=\"../icons/spacer_2.gif\"\n  border=\"0\" alt=\"\"></td>\n<td><img src=\"../icons/blank_4.gif\"\n  border=\"0\" alt=\"\"></td>\n</tr></table>\n<b class=\"navlabel\">Previous:</b> <a class=\"sectref\" HREF=\"node270.html\">33.1 Atoms, Bond, Conformers,</A>\n<b class=\"navlabel\">Up:</b> <a class=\"sectref\" HREF=\"node269.html\">33.
