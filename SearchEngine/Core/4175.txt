All materials as outlined below should be submitted <strong>before the December 15th deadline</strong>. (Additional information for those re-applying to the Bren School can be found <a href=\"Prospective_Re-applying.php\" target=\"_blank\" title=\"ICS Student Affairs: Re-applying for Graduate Program\">here</a>.)</p>\n<p>You\ncan still apply to the Bren School if your undergraduate degree is not in the\nsame area as the graduate degree in which you are interested. However, it is\nhelpful if you have taken courses in computer science and math, and/or have\nsome related work experience.</p>\n<p><em>ICS accepts applications for admission beginning in the fall quarter only</em>.</p>\n<hr style=\"width: 100%;\" width=\"100%\"/>\n<p><strong><span style=\"color: #6699cc;\"><strong>&#187;</strong></span> Online Application</strong></p>\n<p>Complete the <a href=\"https://apps.grad.uci.edu/ogsa//index.cfm?action=log_in&amp;CFID=13244617&amp;CFTOKEN=90684455\" target=\"_blank\" title=\"Applicaton for Graduate Admission\">online application</a>. Pay particular attention to the following:</p>\n<ul>\n</ul>\n<ul>\n<li>Enter the appropriate codes for ICS. Note that <em>you must choose either M.S. or Ph.D.</em> as ICS does not have a M.S./Ph.D. program.</li>\n</ul>\n<blockquote>\n<ul>\n<li type=\"circle\">Computer Science, <strong>M.S.</strong></li>\n<li type=\"circle\">Computer Science, <strong>Ph.D.</strong></li>\n<li type=\"circle\">Informatics, <strong>Ph.D.</strong></li>\n<li type=\"circle\">Information and Computer Science, <strong>M.S.</strong></li>\n<li type=\"circle\">Networked Systems, <strong>M.S.</strong></li>\n<li type=\"circle\">Networked Systems, <strong>Ph.D.</strong></li>\n<li type=\"circle\">Software Engineering, <strong>M.S.</strong></li>\n<li type=\"circle\">Software Engineering, <strong>Ph.D.</strong></li>\n<li type=\"circle\">Statistics, <strong>M.S.</strong></li>\n<li type=\"circle\">Statistics, <strong>Ph.D.</strong></li>\n</ul>\n</blockquote>\n<ul>\n<li>Enter a specific area of interest. More information about research areas can be found here.</li>\n<li>Save your application ID and student ID numbers; they will be asked of you when filling out other documents.</li>\n<li>Applications are processed after the application fee ($90 for domestic applicants, $110 for foreign applicants) has been received. The application fee canot be waived.</li>\n<li>If you have questions regarding your electronic application or your payment, contact UCI Graduate Division at (949) 824-4611 or ogs@ucs.edu.</li>\n</ul>\n<p>&#160;</p>\n<p><strong><span style=\"color: #6699cc;\"><strong>&#187;</strong></span> GRE and TOEFL Scores</strong></p>\n<p>Arrange to have your official test scores sent from the testing agency\nto UC Irvine. Photocopies or scans will not be accepted.</p>\n<p><span style=\"text-decoration: underline;\">GRE</span></p>\n<ul>\n<li>The General GRE is required of ALL applicants.</li>\n<li>For official GRE General test scores, use <strong>Institutional code: 4859</strong></li>\n<li>There is no minimum GRE score.</li>\n<li>The Computer Science Subject GRE is recommended for applicants who do\nnot have a degree in Computer Science or a related field.</li>\n<li>GRE scores are good for five years, after which time the exam must be\nretaken.</li>\n<li>We cannot accept GMA scores in lieu of GRE scores.</li>\n</ul>\n<blockquote>\n<ul>\n</ul>\n</blockquote>\n<p><span style=\"text-decoration: underline;\">TOEFL</span></p>\n<ul>\n<li>The TOEFL (Test of English as a Foreign Language) is required of\ninternational applicants.</li>\n<li>For official TOEFL test scores, use <strong>Institutional code: 4859</strong></li>\n<li>The minimum TOEFL score is 80. For more information, please see\n<a href=\"http://www.grad.uci.edu/admissions/applying-to-uci/english-proficiency.html\" target=\"_blank\" title=\"Graduate Division English Proficiency\">Graduate Division's English Proficiency site</a>.</li>\n<li>UCI\nwill only waive the TOEFL requirement for applicants who have completed ALL\nrequirements for a B.S., M.S. or Ph.D. degree in the U.S. prior to submitting\ntheir application.</li>\n</ul>\n<blockquote>\n<ul>\n</ul>\n</blockquote>\n<p>&#160;</p>\n<p><strong><span style=\"color: #6699cc;\"><strong>&#187;</strong></span> Letters of Recommendation</strong></p>\n<p><em>Three</em> letters of recommendation are required, but you may submit as\nmany letters as you wish.</p>\n<p><span style=\"text-decoration: underline;\">For paper submission</span></p>\n<p style=\"padding-left: 30px;\">Provide the Letter of Recommendation and Waiver of Access forms to at\nleast three people you have identified who can evaluate your academic and/or\nprofessional achievements, describe your strengths and weaknesses and comment\non your character, integrity and motivation. Your recommenders should mail his/her letter directly to:</p>\n<p style=\"padding-left: 50px;\">ICS Graduate Office<br/>Donald Bren School of Information and Computer Sciences<br/>352 Information &amp; Computer Science Bldg.<br/>University of California, Irvine<br/>Irvine, CA 92697-3430</p>\n<p><span style=\"text-decoration: underline;\">For online submission</span></p>\n<p style=\"padding-left: 30px;\">Applicants who have completed and submitted their online application\nshould send their recommenders a link to that page, along with their\napplication ID number.</p>\n<p>&#160;</p>\n<p><strong><span style=\"color: #6699cc;\"><strong>&#187;</strong></span> Personal Statement</strong></p>\n<p>The\nPersonal Statement (same as Statement of Purpose) should be submitted via the\nonline application.</p>\n<p>&#160;</p>\n<p><strong><span style=\"color: #6699cc;\"><strong>&#187;</strong></span> Official Transcripts</strong></p>\n<p>Arrange to have one copy of your official transcripts from the colleges\nyou attended sent directly to the ICS Graduate Office.</p>\n<p>An official English translation is required from international\nstudents.</p>\n<p>Photocopies, scans, or unofficial copies of transcripts will not be\naccepted.</p>\n<p>&#160;</p>\n<hr style=\"width: 100%;\" width=\"100%\"/>\n<p><strong><span style=\"color: #6699cc;\"><strong>&#187;</strong></span> Checking Your Application Status</strong></p>\n<p>Applicants may login to the online <a href=\"https://gats.ics.uci.edu//tracker/\" target=\"_blank\" title=\"GATS Tracker\">GATS TRACKER</a> to check for\napplication materials received and their current application status.</p>\n<p>Ph.D. and M.S. admissions decisions are ongoing from approximately\nMarch through May. Acceptance and denial letters will be sent electronically.</p>\n<p>We cannot provide you with the final decision over the phone.</p>\n\n\n</div>\n</blockquote>\n</div>\n<div id=\"sidebar\"><div id=\"sidebar_title\">More Graduate &#187;</div>\n<ul>\n<li><a href=\"index.php\">Prospective students</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"../degrees/index.php\">Programs of study</a>\n<ul>\n<li><a href=\"../degrees/degrees_MSOverview.php\">M.S. degree overview</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"../degrees/degrees_PhDOverview.php\">Ph.D. degree overview</a></li>\n</ul>\n</li>\n<li><a href=\"../courses/index.php\">Academic year plan</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"../forms/index.php\">Forms</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"../policies/index.php\">Policies</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"../funding/index.php\">Funding &amp; housing</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"../../../computing/account/new.php\">Computing support</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"../resources.php\">Campus resources</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"../../about/visit/index.php\">Visit the Bren School</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"../../bin/pdf/grad/Graduate Student Handbook F14-15.pdf\">Graduate Student Handbook</a></li>\n</ul></div>\n<div id=\"footer\"><a href=\"http://www.uci.edu/copyright.php\">Copyright Inquiries</a> |\n   <a href=\"http://www.uci.edu/cgi-bin/phonebook\">UCI Directory</a> |\n   <a href=\"http://intranet.ics.uci.edu/\">Intranet</a> |\n   \n   <!-- <a href=\"http://www.ics.uci.edu/sitemap.php\">Site Map</a> | -->\n \n  <a href=\"mailto: i%63%73%77%65%62m%61s%74%65r@%69c%73%2e%75c%69%2eedu\">icswebmaster</a></div>\n</div>\n<script type=\"text/javascript\">\nvar gaJsHost = ((\"https:\" == document.location.protocol) ? \"https://ssl.\" : \"http://www.\");\ndocument.write(unescape(\"%3Cscript src='\" + gaJsHost + \"google-analytics.com/ga.js' type='text/javascript'%3E%3C/script%3E\"));\n</script>\n<script type=\"text/javascript\">\nvar pageTracker = _gat._getTracker(\"UA-605898-1\");\npageTracker._trackPageview();\n</script>\n</body>\n</html>", "id": 4175.0}
