   \t\t\t<p>I always believed that visual means will dominate Web not in too distant a future.  <a href=\"http://www.nytimes.com/2009/01/18/business/media/18ping.html?th&#038;emc=th\">This article in NYT heralds </a>this trend very nicely:</p>\n<blockquote><p>Tyler\u00e2\u20ac\u2122s way of experiencing the Web \u00e2\u20ac\u201d primarily through video \u00e2\u20ac\u201d may not be mainstream, at least not yet. But his use of YouTube as his favorite search engine underscores a shift that is much broader than the quirky habits of children.</p>\n<p>..</p>\n<p>With inexpensive cameras flooding the market and a proliferation of Web sites hosting seemingly unlimited numbers of clips, it\u00e2\u20ac\u2122s never been easier to create and upload video. You can now find an online video on virtually any topic. Web videos teach how to grout a tub, offer reviews of the latest touch-screen phones and give you a feel for walking across the Ponte Vecchio in Florence, Italy.</p>\n</blockquote>\n<p>There is a question in this piece:</p>\n<blockquote><p>As more video is added to the Web, the proportion of video searches that deliver satisfactory answers will grow, too. The question is, how far will video go as an alternative to text?</p>\n</blockquote>\n<p>This is a &#8216;wrong&#8217; question.  Video should not be viewed as an alternative.  Video and text are complementary &#8212; they help each other.  As our five senses work together &#8212; they are not suppose to be alternative to any other sense, and to our brain, video and text should not be treated as alternatives.  The right question is how will they enhance our experience when both of them can be used equally easily.  At one time text dominated because of the technolgy limitations.  As technology makes it easier to use video, they will be used creatively to enhance our overall experience.</p>\n  \t\t\t</div>\r\n  \t\t\t<div class=\"entry-meta-bar clearfix\">\t        \t\t\t\r\n    \t\t\t<div class=\"entry-meta\">\r\n    \t\t\t\t<span class=\"by-author\">By <a href=\"http://ngs.ics.uci.edu/author/ramesh/\">Ramesh</a></span> |\r\n    \t\t\t\t<span class=\"date\"><a href=\"http://ngs.ics.uci.edu/text-or-video-no-it-should-be-text-and-video/\" title=\"2:43 pm\">January 19, 2009</a></span> |\r\n    \t\t\t\t             \t\t<span class=\"category\"><a href=\"http://ngs.ics.uci.edu/category/experiential-computing/\" rel=\"category tag\">Experiential Computing</a></span> |\r\n             \t \r\n    \t\t\t\t             \t\t<span class=\"comments\"><a href=\"http://ngs.ics.uci.edu/text-or-video-no-it-should-be-text-and-video/#comments\" title=\"Comment on Text or Video &#8212;
