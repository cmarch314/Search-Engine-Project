width=\"200\">\n\n<p>Consider the image patches at right. Though they lack global context, it is\noften clear which contain object boundaries and which do not. We are developing\nalgorithms that integrate local image measurements in order to provide global\nexplanations of scenes in terms of objects, surfaces and their spatial\nrelations. This includes algorithms for bottom-up boundary detection and\nsegmentation from local features as well integrating top-down knowledge such as\nthe detection of familiar objects.\n\n\n      </div>\n
