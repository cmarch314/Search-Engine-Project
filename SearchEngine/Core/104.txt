MacBook Airs and iPads also are available for checkout to students enrolled in a Bren School major who are in good academic standing. \n</p>\n<p>\nThe lab is located in ICS2, Room 110.</p>\n<h3>Lab Availability and Policies</h3>\n<p>\nAccess to the Kay Family Foundation Innovation Lab will be granted on a quarterly basis to students in a Bren School of ICS major. Students must request access from the Bren School facilities via \n<a href=\"http://keys.ics.uci.edu\">keys.ics.uci.edu</a>, specifying Mary Stasik as Supervisor. \nCodes will open the lab between 7 a.m. and 10 p.m. daily. Codes will expire at the end of each quarter.</p>\n<p><span style=\"color: red;\"><strong>Important Guidelines: </strong></span>\n</p>\n<ul>\n<li>Students are not to share their code or prop the door open, at the risk of losing room privileges for a full quarter. \n   </li>\n<li>Food or drinks (including water) are not allowed in the lab, and anybody found with food/drink in the room will lose room privileges for a full quarter.  \n</li>\n</ul>\n<p>\nThe lab will be monitored via periodic checks by ICS Student Council members who helped design the lab and develop these policies. The ICS Student Council has been tasked with supporting the Bren School in its enforcement of these rules.  \n</p>\n<p>\nPeriodically, the lab may be reserved for a class or seminar. <span>Please check </span><span style=\"font-family: Verdana,Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif;\"><span>if the room is available for use (or booked). <a href=\"http://exchange.uci.edu/owa/calendar/ICS_ICS2-110@exchange.uci.edu/Calendar/calendar.html\">Check Schedule </a></span></span></p>\n<a href=\"http://exchange.uci.edu/owa/calendar/ICS_ICS2-110@exchange.uci.edu/Calendar/calendar.html\">\n</a>\n<h3>MacBook Air &amp; iPad Checkout Policy</h3>\n<p>\nStudents must be enrolled in a Bren School major and must be in good academic standing in order to check out a MacBook Air or an iPad. These computers may be checked out for one week at a time &#8212; one device per student &#8212; via online reservation only. \n</p>\n<ul>\n<li>RESERVATIONS&#160;<br/>\n      You must reserve a computer in advance at: &#160; &#160; &#160; &#160;<br/><a href=\"http://support.ics.uci.edu/comp_checkout\">http://support.ics.uci.edu/comp_checkout</a></li>\n<li>PICKUP<br/>\n      When the laptop or tablet you&#8217;ve requested is available, you will be notified via email and granted two sessions to pick up the device from ICS 346 before your request is cancelled. Pickup days/times are as follows:\n      \n<ul>\nMonday 1 to 2 p.m. <br/>\n         Friday 11 a.m. to noon\n      \n</ul>\n</li>\n<li>LATE CHARGE<br/>\n      If you return the MacBook Air or iPad past the due date, you will be charged a $10 fee for each day the computer is late, up to one week. If the computer is not returned by seven days past the due date, the value of the device will be added to your UCI student Zot Account in the Registrar&#8217;s Office.\n   </li>\n<li>DAMAGES<br/>\n      If the device sustains any damage while you have it checked out, you will be responsible for the cost of repair or replacement.\n</li>\n</ul>\n\n\n</div>\n</blockquote>\n</div>\n<div id=\"sidebar\"><!-- start right hand side bar -->\n<div id=\"sidebar_title\">More about the school &#187;</div>\n<ul>\n<li><a href=\"../about_mission.php\">Bren ICS Mission</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"../bren/index.php\">About the Bren gift</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"../about_deanmsg.php\">Dean's welcome</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"http://intranet.ics.uci.edu/\">Bren School Intranet</a></li>\n<!--\n<li><a href=\"/ICS/ics/about/about_facts\">Facts and figures</a></li>\n-->\n<li><a href=\"../brenhall/index.php\">Donald Bren Hall</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"../../ugrad/index.php\">Undergraduate education</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"../../grad/index.php\">Graduate studies</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"../search/index.php\">Find faculty and staff</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"../about_contact.php\">Contact us</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"../videos/index.php\">iTunes U</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"../about_safety.php\">Safety procedures</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"../visit/index.php\">Visit the Bren School</a></li>\n</ul>\n<ul>\n</ul></div>\n<div id=\"footer\"><a href=\"http://www.uci.edu/copyright.php\">Copyright Inquiries</a> |\n   <a href=\"http://www.uci.edu/cgi-bin/phonebook\">UCI Directory</a> |\n   <a href=\"http://intranet.ics.uci.edu/\">Intranet</a> |\n   \n   <!-- <a href=\"http://www.ics.uci.edu/sitemap.php\">Site Map</a> | -->\n \n  <a href=\"mailto: i%63%73%77%65%62m%61s%74%65r@%69c%73%2e%75c%69%2eedu\">icswebmaster</a></div>\n</div>\n<script type=\"text/javascript\">\nvar gaJsHost = ((\"https:\" == document.location.protocol) ?
