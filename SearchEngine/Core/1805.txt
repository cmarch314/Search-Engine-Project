equipment</a></h3>\n<div class=\"level3\">\n\n</div>\n\n<h3><a name=\"travel_expense_report\" id=\"travel_expense_report\">Travel expense report</a></h3>\n<div class=\"level3\">\n\n<p>\nThe travel expense report form to is pink and you can get one on the 3rd floor of DBH, in the room in the middle of the floor. You can fill out the whole form or you can just fill out your name and break down your expenses on a separate excel sheet (to make expense calculations, currency conversion, summing, etc.) easier. Expenses should be reported in dollars, even if the receipt is in some other currency. Meals expenses: within the US, exact amounts must be reported, but for travel abroad, you can claim the per diem amount.\n</p>\n\n<p>\nReceipts: you need to provide a credit card statement for airfare and a receipt or registration confirmation with the last 4 credit card digits must be provided for the conference registration.\n</p>\n\n</div>\n\n    <!-- end rendered wiki content -->\n    <div
