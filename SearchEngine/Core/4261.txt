on duty is a greater deterrent than\r\n            a few cameras (although we reserve the right to supplement the Assistants\r\n            with cameras later). Lab Assistants\r\n            do not know everything there is to know about computers. Lab Assistants\r\n            do attempt to deal with Lab problems or report the problems to someone\r\n            who can deal with them. Lab Assistants do activate ICS computer accounts\r\n            and maintain printers. CS 364 serves as an Open Lab\r\n            almost exclusively.</p>\r\n        </blockquote>\r\n        <h5><strong>Q:</strong> <a name=\"games\"></a>Can I play games on the Lab\r\n          computers?</h5>\r\n        <blockquote>\r\n          <p>This may not directly answer the question, but know these things:\r\n            Instructional purposes always have priority.
