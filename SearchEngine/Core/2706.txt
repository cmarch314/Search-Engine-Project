/></h2>\n<h2><strong>iCVideo - Interactive Collaborative Video</strong></h2>\n<p align=\"justify\"><span class=\"left\"><b>Overview:</b> The REAQTIVE paradigm aims to make optimal use of an ensemble of limited resource low-quality projection-enabled multimedia devices  to produce a higher quality viewing experience than is not possible with a single such device. A group of users partaking in a multimedia session such as watching a video, or sharing a multimedia presentation can aggregate a group of low cost/quality devices that are designed and assembled to work together in a reconfigurable environment to realize an improved viewing experience.   </span></p>\n<p align=\"justify\">Pico projectors are destined to become very popular devices in the mobile market. Their integration into wireless devices (cell phones, PDAs) will open up a whole new spectrum of possibilities in the portable multi media domain. However, there are several tradeoffs involved. A close analysis of projector trends reveals a strong correlation between power consumption and portability and output quality. High quality audio and video demands more power reducing battery longevity.</p>\n<p align=\"justify\">REAQTIVE is a new paradigm of resource management, device synchronization and video coding/decoding for an ensemble of portable multimedia devices to collaboratively deliver an enhanced viewing experience for the end users. Enhanced in this context refers to frame rate, image resolution and brightness, audio quality, and power savings (for longer viewing ability).</p>\n<p>Publications:</p>\n<table border=\"0\"
