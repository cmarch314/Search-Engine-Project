\t\t\t\tEvents in Singapore    \t\t\t</h1><!-- .entry-title -->\r\n  \t\t\t</header>\r\n\r\n  \t\t\t\r\n  \t\t\t\r\n  \t\t\t<div class=\"entry-content clearfix\">\r\n    \t\t\t<p>Seraja was used for two major events in Singapore:\u00c2\u00a0 <a title=\"idm\" href=\"http://www.seraja.com/event/590859\">IDM Jamboree </a>and <a title=\"mmm\" href=\"http://www.seraja.com/event/590812\">International Conference on Multimedia Modeling</a>.\u00c2\u00a0 This is the first time that this system is used for such large events where the events went through their complete cyscle &#8212; posting and planning, event, and post event activities.\u00c2\u00a0 Posting included blogging, posring photos, videos, powerpoint, and comments &#8212; many times live.\u00c2\u00a0 Using this platform, even remote users could ask questions in a session to a speaker.</p>\n<p>As the technology matures and such platform become common, the nature of conference proceedings will change.\u00c2\u00a0 Currently, once the conference is over, there is very little, except memories\u00c2\u00a0with attendees, is carried forward.\u00c2\u00a0 This platform allows an event to remain available for experiencing as long as required.\u00c2\u00a0\u00c2\u00a0Do visit to see some of this for at\u00c2\u00a0the following locations for</p>\n<p>IDM Jamboree: <a href=\"http://www.seraja.com/event/590859\">http://www.seraja.com/event/590859</a>\u00c2\u00a0</p>\n<p>MMM Conference: <a href=\"http://www.seraja.com/event/590812\">http://www.seraja.com/event/590812</a></p>\n<p>\u00c2\u00a0</p>\n<p>There you will find that the events are organized hierarchically &#8212; each event has many subevents which may further have many sub events.\u00c2\u00a0 Finally for each event there are different types of reports (audio, video, photos, text) and people could continue commenting &#8212; asking questions and getting answers.</p>\n<p>This is starting an event with a multimedia blog and then converting it to a multimedia wiki.\u00c2\u00a0 This was the first time and Seraja did it at such a short notice knowing well that the system is really in its early beta.\u00c2\u00a0</p>\n<p>Congratulations to all who participated in this experiment in various ways.\u00c2\u00a0 This was an exciting event.\u00c2\u00a0 My only regret is that I had to follow it from about 10,000 miles (and 16 hour time difference &#8212; many of the events are over about 10 hours ago on the same day when I\u00c2\u00a0am posting this)\u00c2\u00a0away.\u00c2\u00a0 The fact that I could follow this and see some photos posted live is very exciting, however.</p>\n  \t\t\t</div>\r\n  \t\t\t<div
