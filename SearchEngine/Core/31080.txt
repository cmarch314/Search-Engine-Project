A view can only be created if\nthe viewer supports the MIME type of the object. Typically, a view will\nmanifest as a graphical depiction of the object on the user's display. Alternatively,\nthe viewer may hide specific access to commands from the user, and instead\ninvoke hypermedia commands based on a user's actions within the view.
