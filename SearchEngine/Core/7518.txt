This examination should evaluate both general preparedness in the discipline, \nand specific competence to pursue the proposed dissertation topic. \n</p>\n<p>In its deliberation, the committee ordinarily will review the student's \nacademic record, preliminary examinations, evaluations by other faculty, and may \nconduct any other examination it thinks appropriate. The candidacy committee will also review an outline of the proposed \ndissertation project, and will determine by oral examination the student's \ncompetence in that area. When, by unanimous vote, the committee decides the student is qualified for \nthe dissertation phase, it should recommend advancement to candidacy to the \nGraduate council via the Graduate Dean. \n</p>\n<p>Following its formal appointment, the committee is free to adopt whatever \nprocedures it thinks appropriate to conduct the qualifying examination for \ncandidacy, subject to the rules of the program and those specified below. Administration of the qualifying examination must conform to the policies \nestablished by the Graduate Council.</p>\n<ul>\n<li>The student must be given adequate notice of the content, form and time of \nthe examination. The committee must meet to decide upon the procedures to be \nfollowed, and the student must be given an opportunity to comment upon the \nselected procedures.</li>\n<li>Before voting upon its recommendation for or against candidacy, the committee \nas a whole shall meet with the student, and any member of the committee will \nhave the right to pose appropriate questions to the student.</li>\n<li>If it decides to do so, the committee may conduct part of the examination on \nan individual basis, e.g. the student may meet with each in turn. However, the committee must conclude its examination when convened with the student present.</li>\n<li>Although the formal candidacy examination ordinarily is conducted in a single \nday, the committee may meet intermittently over a longer period, and may decide \nto reexamine the student on one or more topics after a specified interval.</li>\n<li>When the committee meets to conduct the oral candidacy examination, it must \nreport to the Graduate Council via the Graduate Dean within 30 days.</li>\n<li>If the committee decides to reexamine the student at a later date or does not \npass the student for any reason, this must be reported. The final vote and \nrecommendation of the committee must be unanimous and unequivocal.</li>\n</ul>\n<p>Upon completion of the candidacy examination, the results should be submitted \nto the UCI Graduate Division on the Report of the Ph.D. Candidacy Committee, \nPh.D. Form I. This form must be signed by all committee members at the time the \ncandidacy examination is concluded, and submitted even if the examination was \nfailed. \n</p>\n<p>If the unanimous recommendation of the committee is favorable, the $90 advancement to candidacy fee must be paid by the student to the campus Cashier's \nOffice. This will validate the Ph.D. Form I, after which it should be submitted \nto the UCI Graduate Division. The signed and validated Ph.D. Form I serves as \nthe application for advancement to candidacy. \n</p>\n<p>The candidate and graduate counselor will be notified of formal advancement \nand the appointment of a doctoral committee. </p>\n\n\n</div>\n</blockquote>\n</div>\n<div id=\"sidebar\"><div id=\"sidebar_title\">More Graduate &#187;</div>\n<ul>\n<li><a href=\"../admissions/index.php\">Prospective students</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"../degrees/index.php\">Programs of study</a>\n<ul>\n<li><a href=\"../degrees/degrees_MSOverview.php\">M.S. degree overview</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"../degrees/degrees_PhDOverview.php\">Ph.D. degree overview</a></li>\n</ul>\n</li>\n<li><a href=\"../courses/index.php\">Academic year plan</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"../forms/index.php\">Forms</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"index.php\">Policies</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"../funding/index.php\">Funding &amp; housing</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"../../../computing/account/new.php\">Computing support</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"../resources.php\">Campus resources</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"../../about/visit/index.php\">Visit the Bren School</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"../../bin/pdf/grad/Graduate Student Handbook F14-15.pdf\">Graduate Student Handbook</a></li>\n</ul></div>\n<div id=\"footer\"><a href=\"http://www.uci.edu/copyright.php\">Copyright Inquiries</a> |\n   <a href=\"http://www.uci.edu/cgi-bin/phonebook\">UCI Directory</a> |\n   <a href=\"http://intranet.ics.uci.edu/\">Intranet</a> |\n   \n   <!-- <a href=\"http://www.ics.uci.edu/sitemap.php\">Site Map</a> | -->\n \n  <a href=\"mailto: i%63%73%77%65%62m%61s%74%65r@%69c%73%2e%75c%69%2eedu\">icswebmaster</a></div>\n</div>\n<script type=\"text/javascript\">\nvar gaJsHost = ((\"https:\" == document.location.protocol) ?
