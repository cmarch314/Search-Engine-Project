size=\"3\" /><font face=\"Times New Roman\"><strong>Insights and Experiences</strong>: The third major evolutionary stage is being propelled by the easy availability and utilization of multimodal sensory data and devices with ability to capture, play, store and process sensory data.\u00c2\u00a0 This phase will bring Insights and Experience to the main stage in a similar way as the last phase brought information and communication.\u00c2\u00a0 Information is important to implement decisions.\u00c2\u00a0 To make decisions one requires insights.\u00c2\u00a0 And insights are closely correlated to experiences.\u00c2\u00a0 Experience is defined as <em>active participation in events or activities, leading to the accumulation of knowledge or skill</em>. \u00c2\u00a0\u00c2\u00a0Humans experience events and activities using their sensory tools: sight, sound, touch, smell, and taste.\u00c2\u00a0 Sensing technology has resulted in efficient approaches for converting sight, sound, and touch to electronic form and then back for human perception.\u00c2\u00a0 Smell and taste remain relatively difficult.\u00c2\u00a0 Fortunately, sight and sound are two major senses used by humans in experiencing the world around them and creating knowledge of their environment.\u00c2\u00a0 This stage will help in augmenting cognitive capabilities of humans.\u00c2\u00a0 This could lead to several revolutionary approaches for expanding the results of computing to that part of the world, more than 80% population, which has remained relatively untouched by computing so far.</font></p>\n<p><font face=\"Times New Roman\" size=\"3\" /></p>\n<p><font face=\"Times New Roman\" size=\"3\">In the following table, the basic features and characteristics of the three stages of this evolution are
