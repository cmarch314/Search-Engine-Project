few words of explanation</p>\r\n\r\n<p>In order to understand the results as presented, here is an explanation of how the tournament was structured:</p>\r\n\r\n<ul>\r\n  <li>Any AI that didn't compile was immediately thrown out.</li>\r\n  <li>For all pairs of AIs <i>i</i> and <i>j</i>, <i>i</i> played two games against <i>j</i>: once with <i>i</i> as the black player and once with <i>i</i> as the white player.</li>\r\n  <li>Any AI that threw an exception, returned an invalid move, crashed, threw an exception from its chooseMove() function, or took too long to make a move (3 CPU seconds on my machine) was immediately disqualified and was not allowed to play any more games.</li>\r\n  <li>After all the games were played, the results of all games between pairs of AIs that were <i>not</i> disqualified were tabulated.  Any games played by an AI that was subsequently disqualified were thrown out.  As a result, the number of games counted for each AI was the same.  There were 91 AIs that were not disqualified.  Each of them played 180 games (two games against each of the other 90 qualifying AIs).</li>\r\n  <li>The order of finish in the tournament was determined by two factors:\r\n    <ul>\r\n      <li>The <i>winning percentage</i>, which was calculated using the formula: <i>(wins + (ties / 2.0)) / games_played</i>.</li>\r\n      <li>The total number of tiles earned in all games played, which was used as a tiebreaker if there was a tie on winning percentage.</li>\r\n    </ul>\r\n  </li>\r\n</ul>\r\n\r\n<p>51 AIs that compiled were disqualified.  Most of these disqualifications were the result of taking too long to make a move, or of choosing a move that was invalid.  A handful of others crashed or threw exceptions.  Two others were disqualified because they didn't compile.</p>\r\n\r\n<p>One more reminder: The results of this tournament are purely for entertainment purposes and will not reflect on your grade.</p>\r\n\r\n</div>\r\n\r\n<div class=\"section\">\r\n\r\n<hr />\r\n\r\n<p class=\"title\">The results</p>\r\n\r\n<p><b>AI Name</b> is the name under which the AI was registered using the ICS46_DYNAMIC_FACTORY_REGISTER macro.  <b>Total Record</b> indicates the AI's total number of wins, losses, and ties in all games.  <b>Tiles For</b> is the total number of tiles the AI earned in all of the games it played, while <b>Tiles Against</b> is the total number of tiles the AI's opponent earned in all of the games it played.
