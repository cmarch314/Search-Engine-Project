We formulate features that\nrespond to characteristic changes in brightness, color, and texture associated\nwith natural boundaries. In order to combine the information from these\nfeatures in an optimal way, we train a classifier using human labeled images as\nground truth. The output of this classifier provides the posterior probability\nof a boundary at each image location and orientation. We present\nprecision-recall curves showing that the resulting detector significantly\noutperforms existing approaches.
