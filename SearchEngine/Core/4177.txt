unofficial transcripts are free of charge) or through\nStudent Access. <em><strong>It is the student&#8217;s responsibility to check grades at the end of\neach quarter.</strong></em></p>\n<p><span style=\"text-decoration: underline;\">Traditional grades (A, B, C, etc&#8230;) </span></p>\n<p style=\"padding-left: 30px;\">Students are expected to maintain a B\naverage (this is an absolute requirement if on a fellowship&#8212;GSR, TA, GAANN,\netc.). In order to receive graduation credit, students must earn a grade of B\nor better in all required courses, core courses and breadth courses. A grade of\nB- or below does not have to be improved and can remain on the transcript if\nthat grade is not going to be counted toward graduation requirements.</p>\n<p><span style=\"text-decoration: underline;\">Pass/No Pass</span></p>\n<p style=\"padding-left: 30px;\">P/NP is for undergraduate students only. Graduate students\nmust receive a letter grade to pass a course. Graduate students should not sign\nup for a course P/NP.</p>\n<p><span style=\"text-decoration: underline;\">Satisfactory/Unsatisfactory</span></p>\n<p style=\"padding-left: 30px;\">S/U is for graduate courses only and is\nconsidered to be a letter grade. Faculty can assign an S/U instead of an A, B,\nC, etc. Students are responsible for asking the instructor if they can receive\nthe S/U option instead of an A, B, C, etc. at the beginning of the quarter.\nPlease keep in mind that the S grade is equal to a B or better and the U grade\nis equal to a B- or below. A student who receives a U grade will have to repeat\nthe course if it is going to count towards degree requirements.</p>\n<p><span style=\"text-decoration: underline;\">Incomplete</span> </p>\n<p style=\"padding-left: 30px;\">The &#8220;I&#8221; grade is reserved for occasions when a student&#8217;s\nwork is satisfactory but is incomplete because of circumstances beyond the\nstudent&#8217;s control, and when the student has been excused in advance from\ncompleting the quarter&#8217;s work. Students may ask the instructor to assign an\n&#8220;I,&#8221; but it may or may not be granted. If granted, a student has up to three\nquarters to complete the work. If the work is not completed and a grade has not\nbeen assigned after three quarters, the &#8220;I&#8221; will turn to an F. Once the work\nhas been completed, the instructor must turn in a grade change report to change\nthe &#8220;I&#8221; into a letter grade. The student should check Student Access often to\nconfirm that the grade has been changed.</p>\n<p style=\"padding-left: 30px;\">Graduate Division will not continue to approve employment for a student\nwho has more than two Incompletes on their transcript.</p>\n<p><span style=\"text-decoration: underline;\">No Report</span> </p>\n<p style=\"padding-left: 30px;\">NR means no grade was reported. This can be the result of a\nvariety of reasons:</p>\n<blockquote>\n<ul>\n<li>The faculty member did not turn the grades in on time</li>\n<li>The faculty member does not recognize the student&#8217;s name on the class\nroster</li>\n<li>The faculty member turned in the wrong grade</li>\n<li>The course&#8217;s grade roster was unreadable\n</li>\n</ul>\n</blockquote>\n<p style=\"padding-left: 30px;\">An NR will turn into an F after one quarter. Students who receive an NR\nshould talk to their instructor immediately. The professor will need to\ncomplete a grade change report.</p>\n\n\n</div>\n</blockquote>\n</div>\n<div id=\"sidebar\"><div id=\"sidebar_title\">More Graduate &#187;</div>\n<ul>\n<li><a href=\"../admissions/index.php\">Prospective students</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"../degrees/index.php\">Programs of study</a>\n<ul>\n<li><a href=\"../degrees/degrees_MSOverview.php\">M.S. degree overview</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"../degrees/degrees_PhDOverview.php\">Ph.D. degree overview</a></li>\n</ul>\n</li>\n<li><a href=\"../courses/index.php\">Academic year plan</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"../forms/index.php\">Forms</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"index.php\">Policies</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"../funding/index.php\">Funding &amp; housing</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"../../../computing/account/new.php\">Computing support</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"../resources.php\">Campus resources</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"../../about/visit/index.php\">Visit the Bren School</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"../../bin/pdf/grad/Graduate Student Handbook F14-15.pdf\">Graduate Student Handbook</a></li>\n</ul></div>\n<div id=\"footer\"><a href=\"http://www.uci.edu/copyright.php\">Copyright Inquiries</a> |\n   <a href=\"http://www.uci.edu/cgi-bin/phonebook\">UCI Directory</a> |\n   <a href=\"http://intranet.ics.uci.edu/\">Intranet</a> |\n   \n   <!-- <a href=\"http://www.ics.uci.edu/sitemap.php\">Site Map</a> | -->\n \n  <a href=\"mailto: i%63%73%77%65%62m%61s%74%65r@%69c%73%2e%75c%69%2eedu\">icswebmaster</a></div>\n</div>\n<script type=\"text/javascript\">\nvar gaJsHost = ((\"https:\" == document.location.protocol) ? \"https://ssl.\" : \"http://www.\");\ndocument.write(unescape(\"%3Cscript src='\" + gaJsHost + \"google-analytics.com/ga.js' type='text/javascript'%3E%3C/script%3E\"));\n</script>\n<script type=\"text/javascript\">\nvar pageTracker = _gat._getTracker(\"UA-605898-1\");\npageTracker._trackPageview();\n</script>\n</body>\n</html>", "id": 4177.0}
