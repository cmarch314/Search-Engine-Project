The Bren Professors are some of the most distinguished\nappointments at UC Irvine. </p>\n<p>\nCandidates should bring an integrative outlook to the discipline, enthusiasm in engaging\nwith professional and business communities and the general public,\ncollaborating with UCI scholars who study issues of information and computing\ntechnology, and support for the development of innovative technologies and\napplications. We envision Bren chair-holders to serve as catalysts on campus to\nestablish educational and research programs that foster an interdisciplinary\nperspective. Accordingly, candidates should not only\nhave a strong disciplinary background with a distinguished record of scholarly\npublications and extramural funding, but also a proven track record of\ninnovation, collaboration, stimulation and leadership in both education and\nresearch.</p>\n<p>\nAppointments will be in the Department of Computer\nScience, Informatics, or Statistics at the rank of senior, distinguished\nprofessor. Scholars doing truly cross-disciplinary research may be jointly appointed\nwith another school at UC Irvine. Four chairs are currently filled. </p>\n<p>\nThe Bren School of ICS has excellent faculty,\ninnovative programs, high quality students and outstanding graduates as well as\nstrong relationships with local and national high tech industry. </p>\n<p>\nAs one of eleven academic units at UC Irvine. an\nindependent school with three departments &#8211; Computer Science, Informatics, and\nStatistics &#8211; the Bren School has a unique perspective that provides a broad\nfoundation from which to build initiatives that explore the full extent of the computing\nand information disciplines. </p>\n<p>\nWith 68 regular-rank faculty members, seven\nfull-time lecturers, approximately 250 doctoral, 150 masters, and 1000\nundergraduate students, ICS is one of the largest computing programs in the\ncountry. Many faculty in the school engage in interdisciplinary research\nthrough various organizations such as the California Institute for\nTelecommunications and Information Technology (Calit2), the Institute for\nGenomics and Bioinformatics (IGB), Institute for Software Research (ISR), to\nname but a few. </p>\n<p>\nOutstanding candidates in all relevant areas and at\nother ranks are encouraged to contact the Dean \n(<a href=\"mailto:icsdean@ics.uci.edu\">icsdean@ics.uci.edu</a>) \n</p>\n<p>UC Irvine \n(<a href=\"http://www.uci.edu/\">http://www.uci.edu</a>)&#160; is one of the\nyoungest UC campuses, yet consistently\nranks among the nation&#8217;s best public universities. UCI is located three miles\nfrom the ocean in southern California with an excellent year-round\nMediterranean climate. The area surrounding campus offers numerous outdoor and\nfine arts opportunities and the public school system in Irvine is ranked one of\nthe highest in the nation.</p>\n<p>&#160;</p>\n<p><i>UCI&#160;is an equal\nopportunity employer committed to excellence through diversity and strongly\nencourages applications from all qualified candidates, including women and\nminorities.
