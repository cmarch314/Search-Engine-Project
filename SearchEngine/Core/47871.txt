\t\t\t\tChina Trip Nov. 2006: 2    \t\t\t</h1><!-- .entry-title -->\r\n  \t\t\t</header>\r\n\r\n  \t\t\t\r\n  \t\t\t\r\n  \t\t\t<div class=\"entry-content clearfix\">\r\n    \t\t\t<p>Now I have attended a very well &#8211; almost perfectly &#8212; organized conference, spent some time as a tourist, and been to a University.</p>\n<p>China is definitely very clean and well organized.\u00c2\u00a0 It does compare well to most countries that I have been to.\u00c2\u00a0 Traffic is disciplined, trash is taken care of, even in very crowded places peole are well behaved.\u00c2\u00a0 Tourist attractions are extremely well managed.\u00c2\u00a0 Hangzhou is a city for tourists and most of them are Chinese tourists in this season.\u00c2\u00a0 So I saw that Chinese people are very well behaved in these places.</p>\n<p>The restaurants that I visited were also very impressive.\u00c2\u00a0 Of course these were one of the better ones and were carefully selected to impress &#8212; and that worked.</p>\n<p>The most impressive visit for me was to the University.\u00c2\u00a0 Zhejiang is considered in the top 10 universities in the country.\u00c2\u00a0 I went to their West Lake campus.\u00c2\u00a0 The university is physically very impressive &#8212; nice very well maintained buildings.\u00c2\u00a0 But the more impressive part was the departments.\u00c2\u00a0 The computer science department is extremely well equipped.\u00c2\u00a0 Modern facilities.\u00c2\u00a0 Some labs are sponsored by companies like Microsoft.\u00c2\u00a0 Demos that I was shown compare to those that I will be shown in any advanced country.\u00c2\u00a0 That is not surprising considering the research output in International places that one sees from China.</p>\n<p>A big surprise to me was the number of doctoral students.\u00c2\u00a0 Prof. Yueting Zhuang has more than 20 in his group more than any in US I know.\u00c2\u00a0 And I was told that it is not very uncommon.\u00c2\u00a0 Discussions with some of them showed that they are doing high quality work.\u00c2\u00a0</p>\n<p>So far it has been an amazing experience.\u00c2\u00a0 I will be remiss if I did not mention that people are really good host.\u00c2\u00a0 They are making me feel very important &#8212; I just hope that it does get out of my head before I board plane to get back home.</p>\n  \t\t\t</div>\r\n  \t\t\t<div
