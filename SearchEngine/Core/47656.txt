suppose to be giving a talk &#8212; I really need to prepare for it &#8212; on Indian Technology and how it may (or may not) play an important role in the Flat WOrld.\u00c2\u00a0 Here is the abstract that I prepared for the talk:</p>\n<blockquote><p><font face=\"Times New Roman\">Technology has made the competitive world flat and created opportunities for ambitious countries to change their fortunes.\u00c2\u00a0 To capitalize on this opportunity, a country must select a technology wave and ride it. Timing is critical.\u00c2\u00a0 A country must time its adoption to coincide with certain inflection points\u00e2\u20ac\u201ddisruptive technologies\u00e2\u20ac\u201dto reap the greatest benefits.\u00c2\u00a0 India is considered an early beneficiary of Information Technology revolution.\u00c2\u00a0 Whether India can utilize its early lead to emerge as a leader in the Flat World remains an intriguing question. \u00c2\u00a0It seems that India is in a great position to ride the wave of new frontier in computing, the experiential computing, but many important steps need to be taken to harness this opportunity.\u00c2\u00a0 In this presentation, first we will discuss the disruptive computing technology that could help India become a leader, and then analyze the unique nature of <em>Indian Technology</em> and what needs to happen in India to capitalize on this unique historical opportunity.</font></p></blockquote>\n<p><font face=\"Times New Roman\">I want to analyze the current Indian scene and understand the role of technology in Indian society in the next few decades.\u00c2\u00a0 It will be fscinating research.\u00c2\u00a0 I will be looking at all sources &#8212;
