class=\"entry-content clearfix\">\r\n    \t\t\t<p>In Ho Chi Minh City I met Prof. Lew Lancaster (from UC Berkeley) and Damian Evans (from U Sydney) and had good discussions about their projects related to cultural history and heritage under <a href=\"http://www.ecai.org/\">www.ECAI.org</a>.\u00c2\u00a0 I was very pleased to see that they consider that event-based representations are the key to representation of history.\u00c2\u00a0 In absence of event-based tools, they were developing geo-spatial-temporal approaches for representation.\u00c2\u00a0 Under ECAI, many countries are creating and sharing cultural data to discover, understand, share, all data and knowledge and make it easily accessible by peole.\u00c2\u00a0 Based on the enthusiasm that I saw among Buddhist monks for creating data using such tools, it is clear that even very religious people are eager to help technology when it really helps them.</p>\n<p>Prof. Lancaster had invited me to meet them and give a talk on my work because he believes that by creating and popularizing event-based tools these tasks could in fact be revolutionized.\u00c2\u00a0 This was a music to my ear &#8212; coming from a person who cares about technology because of its ultimate use, rather than for its own sake like most of we technologists do.\u00c2\u00a0 It is truly amazing to see that most of we technologists care for technology and consider its applications as something that some insignificant developer should worry about.</p>\n<p>Coming back to events and history, in a sense every field ranging from economics or business to biology (including computer science) has history and studying that history is what really enriches that field.\u00c2\u00a0 By developing proper event-based approaches to deal with all these disciplines one could really make studying different fields &#8212; not only their history but the real substance in those fields &#8212; significantly easier and accessible.</p>\n<p>I am very excited after meeting them &#8212; it has further strengthened my belief that this areas needs to be established.</p>\n  \t\t\t</div>\r\n  \t\t\t<div class=\"entry-meta-bar clearfix\">\t
