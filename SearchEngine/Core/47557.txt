Multimedia 2007 is in Augsburg, Germany.\u00c2\u00a0 Augsburg is about 1hour car/train ride from Munich.\u00c2\u00a0 I arrived here yesterday and met some friends from this community.\u00c2\u00a0 Looking forward to the 4 busy days at the conference.\u00c2\u00a0 But before that is a fun day &#8212; when after early tutorials, a group of people will go to the Beerfest, or is it Oktoberfest, for experincing multimedia.</p>\n<p>AUgsburg is a small but beautiful town.\u00c2\u00a0 Like most German towns, it is clean and very beautiful.</p>\n<p>Some of my friends were in Germany for the first time and were very surprised that on Sunday everything is closed.\u00c2\u00a0 We had difficulty finding a restaurant open at8:30 PM and ended up eating dinner at the famous American Bistro, called McDonalds.\u00c2\u00a0 I must say that I tried their potato wedges and chicken wrap for the first time and was not disappointed.</p>\n<p>I hardly got over the jet lag of 15 hours from Singapore to get
