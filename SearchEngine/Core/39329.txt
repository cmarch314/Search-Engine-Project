Seel</EM><BR>\n\n\n<P>\n\n<h2>Summary</h2>\n<P>\n\nNef polyhedra are defined as a subset of the d-dimensional space obtained by\na finite number of set complement and set <A Also, Nef polyhedra are closed under topological unary \nset operations. Given a Nef polyhedron one can determine its interior, its\nboundary, and its closure.\n<P>\n\nAdditionally, a d-dimensional Nef polyhedron has the property, that its boundary\nis a (d-1)-dimensional Nef polyhedron.
