        \r\n<P>\r\nOffered jointly with \r\n<A HREF=\"http://www.eng.uci.edu/\">The Henry Samueli School of Engineering</A>, \r\nthe Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) major provides a unique educational opportunity for students interested in \r\nlearning about both the hardware and software aspects of computers, and the application of computers to real-world \r\nproblems. CSE includes methods of organizing and manipulating information (computer science), as \r\nwell as the design of computers and their components (computer engineering). As such, CSE integrates \r\ninteresting topics in computing devices, networks, software and systems engineering.\r\n<P>\r\nCSE graduates are well-positioned for career opportunities in both academia and industry. Our alumni are employed at \r\nleading companies or continue their studies toward M.S. or Ph.D. degrees at prestigious \r\nuniversities.\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\n<div id=\"expandable\" STYLE=\"margin-top: 2em; \">\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\n<P CLASS=\"section_heading\"><A HREF=\"#\">IS COMPUTER SCIENCE AND ENGINEERING FOR ME?</A> <span></span> </P>\r\n<DIV CLASS=\"section_content\">\r\n<P>\r\nEmbedded devices increasingly drive innovation in consumer goods and products, and often are used in large quantities \r\nto form sensor networks that monitor the weather, environment, or physical structures. Students interested in \r\nlearning how such devices are designed and constructed are encouraged to explore the CSE major.\r\n<P>\r\nGraduates of the program will:\r\n<ul class=\"content-list-large\">\r\n   <LI>demonstrate broad knowledge of computer science and engineering;\r\n   <LI>design, describe and use state-of-the-art hardware/software systems;\r\n   <LI>maintain awareness of contemporary issues in computer science and engineering in a global and societal context, \r\n       and an understanding of the professional and ethical responsibilities of their profession;\r\n   <LI>demonstrate effective oral and written communication.\r\n</UL>\r\n\r\n<!-- -->\r\n<!-- -->\r\n\r\n<p>\r\nFor more information on CSE goals and objectives see\r\n<a
