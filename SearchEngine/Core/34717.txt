Electrical Engineering, <A HREF=\"http://www.rpi.edu/\">Rensselaer\nPolytechnic Institute</A>, May 1989.\n\n<P><U><B>WORK EXPERIENCE</B></U><BR>\n\n<P>\n<B>Chair, <A HREF=\"http://www.ics.uci.edu/pub/ietf/webdav/\">\nWeb Distributed Authoring and Versioning (WebDAV)</A>\nWorking Group, <A HREF=\"http://www.ietf.org/\">Internet Engineering Task\nForce</A>, March 1997-present.</B>\n\nFounded and led this working group to enable Web-based remote\ncollaborative authoring and versioning tools to be broadly\ninteroperable. Assembled a broad coalition of participants from\nindustry and academia, including Microsoft, Netscape, IBM, Novell,\nXerox, Rational, Merant, and OTI. Developed the WebDAV Distributed\nAuthoring Protocol, extensions to the Hypertext Transfer Protocol\n(HTTP) to support remote collaborative authoring, now supported by\nsuch industry-leading applications and servers as Office 2000,\nInternet Explorer 5, Apache, Internet Information Services 5, Exchange\n2000, DAV4J (IBM Alphaworks), and many others. Instrumental in the\nformation of the follow-on <A HREF=\"http://www.webdav.org/dasl/\">DASL</A>\n(DAV Searching and Locating) and\n<A HREF=\"http://www.webdav.org/deltav/\">Delta-V</A> (versioning and configuration management) working groups\nwithin the IETF.<BR>\n\n<P><B>Research Assistant, University of California, Irvine, 1993-present.</B>\nPerformed research for DARPA research projects in the areas of remote\ncollaborative authoring, open hypertext, hypertext\nversioning, software architecture, and software\nenvironments. Participated on several DARPA grant-writing teams.\n\n<P><B>Teaching Assistant, University of California, Irvine, 1992-1993.</B>\nAssisted in the teaching of the undergraduate level Software Engineering\ncourse, and the Software Engineering Project course. Consistently\nearned excellent student reviews.\n\n<P><B>Engineer, <A HREF=\"http://www.raytheon.com/\">Raytheon</A> Equipment\nDivision, 1989-1992.</B> Wrote firmware in C and Ada for the German\nCivilian Air Traffic Control (ATC) System (DERD), and for a prototype\nMicrowave Airplane Landing System (MLS). Adapted air traffic control\nsoftware to create a marketing demonstration of a vessel traffic\ncontrol system. Developed prototype touch panel displays for use\nwith the DERD system. Project highlights include:\n\n<UL>\n\n<LI>Wrote firmware using the Spark safety-critical subset of Ada\nfor an R3000 to monitor the control electronics of a MLS in real-time.\nWrote a C program to test a Phase Shifter Controller board. Debugged\nsystem hardware to the component level while performing integration.\n\n<LI>Wrote a multi-screen textual user interface for a real-time\nmultiprocessor (four 68020 single board computers connected via\na VME bus) simulator of nine radars, six direction finders, and\na flight plan information system for the DERD ATC system. \n\n</UL>\n</P>\n\n<P><B><U>PEER REVIEWED JOURNAL PUBLICATIONS</U></B>\n\n<P>\n<OL>\n<LI> \nKenneth M.
