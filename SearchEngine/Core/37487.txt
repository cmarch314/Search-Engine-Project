Panel-->\n\n<H2><A NAME=\"SECTION0022320000000000000000\">\n20.3.2 OEGetDefaultMass</A>\n</H2>\n\n<P>\nOEGetDefaultMass returns an unsigned integer representing the most\ncommon (or default) isotope for an element.  For elements, such as\nBromine that have two or more almost equally common isotopes, OEChem\nuses the same <i>default</i> mass as MDL.  This value is used when\nconverting between OEChem's internal mass value, OEAtomBase\nGetIsotope, to and from MDL's mass differences.  For atomic number\nzero and values greater than or equal to OEElemNo_MAXELEM, this\nfunction returns zero.
