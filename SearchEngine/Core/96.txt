padding-left: 1em; }\n\n</style>\n<p><b>New student club inspired by maker subculture</b></p>\n<p>\n<!-- -->\n<!-- -->\n<!-- -->\n<!-- -->\n</p>\n<p>\n<a href=\"http://www.flickr.com/photos/brenschool/sets/72157628324560397/show/\"><img alt=\"DAT Space\" border=\"0\" src=\"../../../bin/img/photos/spotlight/datspace.jpg\" width=\"450\"/></a></p>\n<p>With workshop topics ranging from Arduino hacking to espresso brewing, new student organization Design, Art, and Technology Hackerspace (DAT Space) is making good on its promise to provide a student-run physical space where members of the UCI community can meet and work together on creative projects. \n</p>\n<p>\nComputer science and engineering major Vahan Hartooni, who founded the club along with Ivan Check, Rachel Rose Ulgado, and Nicholas LaJeunesse, says DAT Space aims to provide a venue for undergraduates to produce something tangible. &#8220;Our club is inspired by the <a href=\"http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Maker_subculture\">maker</a> community,&#8221; he explains. &#8220;Just imagine the do-it-yourself movement, but geekier and with a drive to create a project that is unique in this world.&#8221; \n</p>\n<p>\nSponsored by the <a href=\"http://luci.ics.uci.edu/\">Laboratory for Ubiquitous Computing and Interaction</a>, DAT Space was founded in fall 2011 with funding from a Multidisciplinary Design Program grant through <a href=\"http://www.calit2.uci.edu/\">Calit2</a> and the <a href=\"http://www.urop.uci.edu/\">Undergraduate Research Opportunities Program</a>. The grant program is designed to help students develop skills and knowledge that will propel them into graduate studies or careers in fields that explore connections between different disciplines. \n</p>\n<p>\nDAT Space&#8217;s inaugural workshop, &#8220;Introduction to Arduino,&#8221; drew undergads, graduate students, researchers and faculty who learned how to program the popular microcontroller. Participants created a device that measured an input (e.g., light, sound or pressure) and did something in response (e.g., beep, light up, tweet). Next, &#8220;Introduction to Embroidery&#8221; had attendees stitching their own yarn designs. \n</p>\n<p>\nThe third workshop, &#8220;<a href=\"http://www.flickr.com/photos/brenschool/sets/72157628324560397/\" title=\"Play Dough Circuits Workshop\">Play Dough Circuits</a>,&#8221; encouraged participants to learn the basics of electronics. Using batteries, a very small amount of wiring and two types of dough (one that conducts and another that insulates), student teams constructed light-up creations &#8212; some unique and others modeled after pop culture icons, like Pikachu and the deadmau5 logo. \n</p>\n<p>\n&#8220;The best part of the play dough circuits workshop? Finally getting my monster guy to light up,&#8221; said fourth-year biology major Dominique Lyakov.  \n</p>\n<p>\nDAT Space wrapped up their fall quarter calendar with &#8220;Caffeine Night,&#8221; in which students shared their knowledge of making stovetop espresso, Persian black tea and malted cinnamon mochas. Check their <a href=\"http://www.datspace.net/\">website</a> for future events and to view photos and videos of past workshops. \n</p>\n<div style=\"float: right; margin: 1em 0;\"> \n &#8212; Reported by Jesus Vargas <br/>\n &#8212; Photo by Peter Huynh\n</div>\n\n\n</div>\n</blockquote>\n</div>\n<div id=\"sidebar\"><div id=\"sidebar_title\">More community &#187;</div>\n<ul>\n<li><a href=\"../../alumni/index.php\">Alumni</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"../../friends/index.php\">Friends</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"http://www.parents.uci.edu/\">Parents</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"../../friends/leadershipcouncil/index.php\">Leadership Council</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"../index.php\">News</a></li>\n<li><a href=\"https://ua-web.uadv.uci.edu/eGiving/\">Give a gift</a></li>\n</ul></div>\n<div id=\"footer\"><a href=\"http://www.uci.edu/copyright.php\">Copyright Inquiries</a> |\n   <a href=\"http://www.uci.edu/cgi-bin/phonebook\">UCI Directory</a> |\n   <a href=\"http://intranet.ics.uci.edu/\">Intranet</a> |\n   \n   <!-- <a href=\"http://www.ics.uci.edu/sitemap.php\">Site Map</a> | -->\n \n  <a href=\"mailto: i%63%73%77%65%62m%61s%74%65r@%69c%73%2e%75c%69%2eedu\">icswebmaster</a></div>\n</div>\n<script type=\"text/javascript\">\nvar gaJsHost = ((\"https:\" == document.location.protocol) ? \"https://ssl.\" : \"http://www.\");\ndocument.write(unescape(\"%3Cscript src='\" + gaJsHost + \"google-analytics.com/ga.js' type='text/javascript'%3E%3C/script%3E\"));\n</script>\n<script type=\"text/javascript\">\nvar pageTracker = _gat._getTracker(\"UA-605898-1\");\npageTracker._trackPageview();\n</script>\n</body>\n</html>", "id": 96.0}
