OrbitalEnergyComparator</h1><br /><br />\n<p>Comparator object for Orbitals.  Ranks orbital energy.</p>\n  <p>Depending on how initialized, it will either rank for occupied \n  orbitals such that the maximum rated one should be the highest occupied \n  molecular orbital (HOMO).
