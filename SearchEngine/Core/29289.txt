his doctoral dissertation under my supervision on automatic summarization of personal photo collections As he states Photo taking and sharing devices e g smart phones digital cameras etc have become extremely popular in recent times Photo enthusiasts today capture moments of their personal lives using these devices This has resulted in huge collections of photos stored in various personal dissertation we present a principled approach for generation of size constrained overview summaries from large personal photo collections Considering the importance of the problem created by advances Collections: Pinaki Sinha&#8217;s Thesis    \t\t\t</h1><!-- .entry-title -->\r\n  \t\t\t</header>\r\n\r\n  \t\t\t\r\n  \t\t\t\r\n  \t\t\t<div class=\"entry-content clearfix\">\r\n    \t\t\t<p>Pinaki Sinha finished his doctoral dissertation (under my supervision) on automatic summarization of personal photo collections.  As he states:</p>\n<blockquote><p>Photo taking and sharing devices (e.g., smart phones, digital cameras, etc) have become<br />\nextremely popular in recent times. Photo enthusiasts today capture moments of their personal<br />\nlives using these devices. This has resulted in huge collections of photos stored in<br />\nvarious personal archives. The exponential growth of online social networks and web based<br />\nphoto sharing platforms have added fuel to this fire. According to recent estimates [46],<br />\nthree billion photos are uploaded on the social network Facebook per month. This photo<br />\ndata overload has created some major challenges. One of the them is automatic generation<br />\nof representative overviews from large photo collections. Manual browsing of photo<br />\ncorpora is not only tedious, but also time inefficient. Hence, development of an automatic<br />\nphoto summarization system is not only a research but also a practical challenge. In this<br />\ndissertation, we present a principled approach for generation of size constrained overview<br />\nsummaries from large personal photo collections.\n</p></blockquote>\n<p>Considering the importance of the problem created by advances in photo related technology, this dissertation addresses<br />\nand makes good progress towards solving an important problem for many people.  Very good dissertation.<br />\nCongratulations, Pinaki, for a job well-done.</p>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t<div class=\"tags\">\r\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\tTagged on: <a
