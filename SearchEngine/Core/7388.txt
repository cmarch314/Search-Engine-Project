The additional\nfunding available is limited to student and/or parent loans, depending on\neligibility and borrowing limits. This\nallowance covers hardware, software, monitor, printer, and extended warranty.</p>\nStudents\nare encouraged to visit or contact the Office of Financial Aid and\nScholarships, 102 Aldrich Hall, by email at <a href=\"mailto:finaid@uci.edu\">finaid@uci.edu</a>, or phone at (949)\n824-8262, for details on the budget increase process and limitations.\n<p><strong><span style=\"color: #6699cc;\"><strong>&#187;</strong></span> Recommended Computer Specifications</strong></p>\n<ul>\n<li>RAM: 4 GB or higher</li>\n<li>Hard drive: 250 GB or more </li>\n<li>Processor: Intel Core i5 or i7, or similar AMD</li>\n<li>Weight: Students are expected to bring their laptops to\nclass so please consider weight if this is a factor for you.</li>\n<li>Screen: When writing/debugging programs, more screen\nspace tends to be very useful.</li>\n<li>Warranty: Extended hardware warranty is recommended.</li>\n</ul>\n<ul>\n</ul>\n<p><em>*If you have a laptop that was purchased within the last 4\nyears, the system should be adequate for lower division work. Your computer should be running an operating\nsystem which will receive security updates and should be minimum 2 GB of RAM\nand have sufficient hard drive space to install required programs. </em></p>\n<p><em>**Recommended, but not required:</em></p>\n<ul>\n<li><em>Laptop cable and lock<br/></em></li>\n<li><em>External monitor, keyboard and mouse</em></li>\n<li><em>USB drive or external hard drive for data back-ups</em></li>\n<li><em>Small black &amp; white laser printer (Printers for use are\navailable throughout campus; nominal fee to print)</em></li>\n</ul>\n<ul>\n</ul>\n<p>If you need to connect from off-campus to UCI's network, check out the <a href=\"http://www.lib.uci.edu/how/connect-from-off-campus.html\" target=\"_blank\" title=\"UCI Libraries Off-Campus Connect\">UCI Libraries' page</a> on <a target=\"_blank\" title=\"UCI Libraries VPN Info\">this</a> topic to get set up.</p>\n<p>&#160;</p>\n<hr style=\"width: 100%;\" width=\"100%\"/>\n<p>&#160;</p>\n<p><strong><span style=\"color: #6699cc;\"><strong>&#187;</strong></span> Computer Use Policies</strong></p>\n<p>All ICS students should make themselves familiar with the Bren school's policies governing computer use.
