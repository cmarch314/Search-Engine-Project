The academic year begins in late September and ends mid-June. The pay period for a full-time lecturer appointment is from July 1 through June 30 (a nine-month service period paid over twelve months.)\n</p>\n<p>\nPrimary responsibilities include teaching six or more (quarter/trimester) courses per year, for majors and non-majors, and curriculum development. Additional administrative duties may be assigned by the Chair of the Department.\n</p>\n<p>\nApplicants should hold a graduate degree in Computer Science or a closely related field (Ph.D. preferred), and have a strong interest, and a proven record of excellence, in teaching undergraduate computer science courses at the university level. Salary will be commensurate with degree and experience.\n</p>\n<p>\nSuccessful applicants will find the Department of Computer Science to be a stimulating, diverse environment conducive to professional growth in teaching. Lecturers are considered an essential part of the faculty and participate in departmental decisions pertaining to education. Irvine, a dynamic Southern California city, is the center of a vibrant and culturally rich area that includes a multitude of other colleges. For more information about the department, visit <a href=\"http://www.cs.uci.edu/\">http://www.cs.uci.edu/</a>.\n</p>\n<p>\nSend cover letter, curriculum vitae, description of teaching experience, teaching evaluations, and the names and contact information for three relevant references.
